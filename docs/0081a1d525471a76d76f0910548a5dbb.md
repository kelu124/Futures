## Summary

Amid a push for workers to return to London offices, many are opting for affordable accommodations like Zedwell Capsule Hotel, which offers compact sleeping pods for £30. The hotel, inspired by Japanese capsule concepts, features nearly 1,000 capsules measuring 1x1x2 meters, providing a cozy alternative to expensive hotels in the city, where rates average £265 per night. Zedwell targets young professionals and hybrid workers as a base in the city, especially as companies enforce return-to-office policies. The hotel combines budget hostel amenities with a private sleeping experience, appealing to those looking for affordable options without compromising on comfort. Despite being under construction, it offers amenities like shared showers, vending machines, and plans for improved facilities.
