## Summary

Mining startup Earth AI has announced its discovery of significant deposits of critical minerals in Australia, following a major copper find by another startup, KoBold. Earth AI's founder Roman Teslyuk emphasizes that technological advancements, rather than geography, drive the real frontier in mining. Utilizing field data and artificial intelligence, Earth AI has identified copper, cobalt, gold, silver, molybdenum, and tin deposits in various locations. Despite initial hesitations from potential customers regarding the technology's reliability, Earth AI has developed its own drilling equipment to validate its predictions. The company aims to revolutionize the lengthy mineral exploration process by using efficient AI algorithms, highlighting the need for faster solutions in modern exploration.
