## Summary

The article discusses various contemporary issues, highlighting how our perception of history can make us feel insignificant and helpless. It reflects on the implications of technology, particularly the impact of unlimited smartphone use on children's cognitive development, leading to myopia and reduced attention spans. Additionally, it notes the paradox of greater efficiency in accounting due to Microsoft Excel, resulting in an increase in the number of accountants. Lastly, it reveals a significant shift in construction spending, with data center construction surpassing office construction for the first time, as remote work alters the demand for physical office spaces.
