## Summary

Menopause is gaining attention as a significant health issue, leading to the emergence of menopause retreats that offer personalized support and alternative therapies for women experiencing this life stage. With an expected market growth to $600 billion by 2025, various companies and wellness destinations are focusing on menopause solutions, including hormone replacement therapy, nutrition, and emotional support. Retreats across Europe and Asia provide a range of therapies to address both physical and emotional challenges associated with menopause. However, there is a call for more clinical research to validate the effectiveness of certain wellness treatments. Overall, these retreats foster community and education, helping women navigate their menopause journey.
