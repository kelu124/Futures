## Summary

The article discusses the emotional toll on children detained at the ICE facility in Dilley, Texas, as revealed through letters they wrote. The letters, which illustrate the children's feelings of being trapped and longing for home, detail their experiences of sadness, fear, and health issues, as well as their desire to return to school and family. The stories emphasize the psychological impact of long detention periods, with children like 9-year-old Susej expressing feelings of being stuck and longing for their former lives. The article also highlights the conditions within the center, including limited access to education, medical care, and appropriate food. Overall, the letters provide a poignant insight into the personal struggles faced by these young detainees, raising awareness of the immigrant detention system's impact on children.
