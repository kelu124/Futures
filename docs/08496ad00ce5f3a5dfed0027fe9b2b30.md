## Summary

The Intelligence Age will enable people to achieve what once seemed impossible, thanks to advancements in AI and deep learning. Society, as a form of collective intelligence, benefits from past generations' contributions, and future generations will harness AI tools to solve complex problems and enhance quality of life. With AI, individuals could have personal assistants and tailored educational resources, leading to unprecedented shared prosperity. However, challenges exist, such as ensuring equitable access to AI technology and navigating its impact on labor markets. Despite potential downsides, the future promises remarkable advancements and opportunities for human creativity and collaboration.
