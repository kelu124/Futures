## Summary

The cybersecurity job market faces significant challenges in 2023, with budget cuts leading to layoffs and a growing disconnect between job forecasts and actual hiring. Despite predictions of job growth, many professionals, including former marketing VP Torsten George, are struggling to find new positions due to reduced employer desperation and increased workload on existing teams. Accurate job market data is crucial for job seekers, who should tailor their resumes, leverage networks, and adopt strategic job-hunting approaches. Key hiring insights reveal that most open positions are concentrated in a few companies, with an emphasis on diversifying hiring practices. The advice from industry leaders underscores the importance of creating inclusive environments and supporting those entering or re-entering the cybersecurity field.
