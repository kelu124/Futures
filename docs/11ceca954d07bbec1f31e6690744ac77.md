## Summary

The text highlights the emergence of "granfluencers" like Lillian Droniak, who are redefining the narratives around aging through vibrant social media presence, particularly on TikTok. Droniak, at ninety-four, uses humor and positivity to engage her audience, blending personal stories with relatable content. The article emphasizes how these older influencers are challenging stereotypes of aging, providing connection, and showcasing the joys of later life. Granfluencers are not only entertaining but also offering a more hopeful perspective on aging, inspiring both younger and older generations.
