## Summary

Codi, a startup founded by Christelle Rohaut and Dave Schuman, has launched an AI-powered platform to automate office management, evolving from its original role of connecting companies with flexible office spaces. The platform aims to streamline office logistics, saving companies significant administrative costs amid the shift towards remote and hybrid work. Codi's CEO highlighted that the AI system coordinates essential tasks, drastically reducing the time spent on administrative duties. The platform has already gained traction, signing 40 clients during its beta phase, and targets legacy management firms as competitors. Codi's vision is to create a self-managing office environment, allowing human talent to focus on workplace culture and growth.
