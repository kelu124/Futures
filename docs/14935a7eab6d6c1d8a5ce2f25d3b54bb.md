## Summary

The text discusses the importance of identifying, growing, and recruiting talent in a rapidly changing world shaped by globalization, technology, and demographic changes. It emphasizes six essential skills for the future, categorized into three individual competencies (Cognition, Creativity, Curiosity) and three connection skills (Collaborate, Communicate, Convince). It asserts that individuals and organizations must develop these skills to thrive. Additionally, when recruiting talent, especially from outside, companies should consider Integration, Integrity, and Impact. The text highlights the necessity for individuals to take responsibility for their own career development, continuously honing their skills to adapt to the evolving landscape.
