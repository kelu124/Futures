## Summary

In an era dominated by AI, the authors of "AI and the Art of Being Human" have released the text as a free AI Companion, allowing users to upload it into any AI platform for creative use. This initiative aims to merge traditional book reading with the capabilities of AI, enabling a more interactive experience that connects with the print version. The Companion is structured into three sections: an introduction for readers, instructions for AI engagement, and the full text formatted as markdown. While testing revealed varying effectiveness among AI models, the authors encourage users to share their experiences and innovative applications of the Companion. The goal is to foster creativity and exploration using AI alongside the original book, thus redefining how literature can be engaged in the AI age.
