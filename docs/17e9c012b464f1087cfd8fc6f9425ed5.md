## Summary

The article discusses the evolution of astrology from a personal encounter with an astrologer a decade ago to the current digital landscape dominated by AI tools like Co-Star. It contrasts traditional astrology readings with modern AI-driven experiences, highlighting how platforms utilize large language models to create personalized astrological insights. The author reflects on the changing nature of spirituality, suggesting that while AI can provide insights and frameworks for understanding emotions, the essence of spirituality remains personal. The piece also debates the validity of astrology, showcasing differing perspectives while emphasizing the unifying desire for frameworks in an evolving technological landscape.
