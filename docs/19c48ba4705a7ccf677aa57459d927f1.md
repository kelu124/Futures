## Summary

This 135th issue of the newsletter "Weak Signals and other Trends" covers various emerging trends and insights. It highlights the impact of deepfakes on cybersecurity jobs, Walmart's shift to electronic labels, and AI's influence on pricing in fast-food chains. The author discusses innovative developments, such as transparent solar cells and genetic engineering in lettuce. Weak signals, which indicate potential changes or risks, are explored, along with concepts like 'chrono-working' for flexible hours. The author reflects on recent events attended, including a Summit of Minds in France and a panel on resilience in communities. The issue also presents intriguing statistics about vehicle accidents and the effects of environmental policies in London.
