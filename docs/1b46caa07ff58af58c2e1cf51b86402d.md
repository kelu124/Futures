## Summary

The article discusses the transformative impact of Generative AI (GenAI) on supply chain management and marketing roles. By 2029, AI assistants will handle market analysis, strategy development, and content creation, allowing marketing managers to shift from data gathering to strategic insight curation. The role evolves into that of a creative director, overseeing AI-generated campaigns while ensuring brand authenticity and cultural sensitivity. Managers will become digital architects, designing AI ecosystems for seamless data flow and real-time adaptation. As AI takes on more decision-making, human skills like empathy and ethical reasoning become essential. Ultimately, the AI revolution is redefining management roles across all business functions, emphasizing the need for ongoing adaptation and a focus on uniquely human qualities.
