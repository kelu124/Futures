## Summary

Meta has developed an AI named Cicero that excels in the strategy game Diplomacy, ranking in the top 10% against human players. Unlike traditional board games, Diplomacy requires negotiation and alliance-building, making it a complex challenge for AI. Cicero combines reinforcement learning for strategic moves with a large language model for communication. Despite occasional errors, it successfully collaborated with human players, highlighting its potential to handle real-world problems that require compromise and negotiation, such as traffic planning and contract discussions.
