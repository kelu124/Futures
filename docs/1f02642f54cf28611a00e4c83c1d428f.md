## Summary

The emergence of AI algorithms like Dall-E and Midjourney is revolutionizing design, particularly in architecture. Dall-E is known for creating whimsical images based on text prompts, while Midjourney provides architects with innovative design opportunities by generating a range of architectural concepts from simple keywords. Designers like Mario Coppola and Niccol√≤ Casas highlight the potential of AI to enhance creativity and streamline the design process, despite concerns about homogenization in aesthetics. The collaboration between architects and AI is seen as a fusion that could transform the role of designers, focusing on intuition and visualization, while also potentially impacting job availability in the field.
