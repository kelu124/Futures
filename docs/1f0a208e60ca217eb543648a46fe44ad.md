## Summary

The article discusses the emergence of innovative AI hardware devices in 2024, marking a shift towards new form factors for AI technology, moving beyond traditional computing devices like laptops and smartphones. Key players include Humane, with its AI Pin that uses voice and gesture commands, and Rewind, introducing the AI Pendant for real-world information retrieval. Meta's smart glasses and Apple's Vision Pro are also cited as significant developments in integrating AI with new hardware. The piece highlights the importance of exploring diverse applications for AI devices, such as proactive assistants and health monitoring clusters, emphasizing a future where AI's capabilities are embodied in new, functional forms.
