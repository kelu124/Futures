## Summary

Futures Lab is a seven-workshop program focused on futures literacy, strategic foresight, and collective exploration, addressing real-world challenges such as sustainable food systems and future lifestyles. Students learn methods like horizon scanning and scenario building, transforming their initial uncertainty into confidence and agency regarding their influence on the future. Evaluations show an increase in hope and imaginative competencies, with participants feeling equipped to challenge dominant narratives and relate futures thinking to their fields of study. The program fosters a collaborative learning environment, aiming to promote long-term futures literacy and societal change. The Futures Lab Playbook serves as a guide for others to implement similar initiatives, contributing to the expansion of futures literacy across communities.
