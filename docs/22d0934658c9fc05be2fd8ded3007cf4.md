## Summary

Amazon is enhancing healthcare accessibility through its offerings, including Amazon Pharmacy, which delivers medications to customers' doors, and the acquisition of One Medical, a tech-driven primary care provider. They are introducing Amazon Clinic, a virtual care service for over 20 common health conditions, allowing customers to connect with licensed telehealth providers via a secure messaging system. This service emphasizes convenience, affordability, and flexibility, with no insurance acceptance currently but eligibility for FSA and HSA. Customers can choose their provider, engage in consultations, and receive personalized treatment plans. Initially available in 32 states, Amazon Clinic aims to expand its reach and ensure customer privacy and data security.
