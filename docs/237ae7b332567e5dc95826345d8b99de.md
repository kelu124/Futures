## Summary

This edition of Exponential View discusses the rapid maturation of AI technologies and their impact on organizational structures, indicating a shift towards smaller teams and increased reliance on AI for both analytical and managerial tasks. The World Bank's updated perspective on industrial policy suggests a more favorable view on targeted economic strategies, particularly in light of changing dynamics in global manufacturing, especially involving China. In scientific fields, AI's role is evolving from merely assisting research to autonomously conducting complex scientific tasks, raising questions about understanding and causality in AI outputs. Additional topics cover AI’s integration in Hollywood, the rise of Texas energy storage, and the societal implications of AI-assisted decision-making. Overall, the newsletter explores the transformative landscape influenced by AI across various sectors.
