## Summary

This article discusses the importance of adapting to the rapidly evolving landscape of artificial intelligence (AI) and its impact on individuals and organizations. It outlines three key steps for thriving in an AI age: Embrace the technology, Adapt business models, and Complement AI with human creativity and skills. The author emphasizes the need for individuals to understand and engage with AI tools while organizations must rethink their strategies to enhance productivity and innovation. The article highlights the significance of developing competencies in cognition, creativity, curiosity, collaboration, communication, and persuasion to succeed in a future where human capabilities complement AI advancements. Ultimately, the narrative conveys a hopeful outlook on the potential benefits of AI when integrated thoughtfully into society.
