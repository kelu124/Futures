## Summary

"Multispecies Cities" is a science fiction anthology released on April 13, 2021, exploring the interconnectedness of humans, animals, and the environment within urban ecosystems. With contributions from twenty-four authors, the anthology presents stories that challenge readers to consider multispecies justice and sustainability in urban life. The narratives feature diverse characters and settings, primarily focusing on the Asia-Pacific region, and address significant issues like justice, inclusion, and ecological balance. The stories range from quirky to serious, offering optimistic visions of future urban spaces where humans coexist harmoniously with nature. As a solarpunk collection, it emphasizes hope and innovative solutions to contemporary environmental challenges.
