## Summary

Horizon Europe Cluster 4 aims to foster competitive technologies in the digital, industry, and space sectors, focusing on data and computing technologies, AI, and the European Green Deal. The EU has allocated 85 million euros to promote AI research and develop unbiased AI systems, alongside investments in IoT and edge computing technologies. An additional 206 million euros will be directed towards enhancing digital competitiveness while supporting Green Deal objectives, including 60 million euros for AI and robotics in resource management. Furthermore, 76 million euros will be invested in projects integrating AI, data, and robotics into various industries. The remaining 70 million euros will support cloud-to-edge server development and advancements in semiconductor and quantum sensing technologies. Interested parties can apply for funding by March 19, 2024, with support available for proposal development.
