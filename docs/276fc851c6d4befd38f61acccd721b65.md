## Summary

In the latest issue of the "Weak Signals and other Trends" newsletter, dated April 10, 2026, the author, Estelle, shares insights gathered during her time in Milan. Key topics include competitive intelligence strategies, the AI boom's impact on various industries, and the rising popularity of 'Punk Health' foods among Chinese youth. The newsletter emphasizes the importance of identifying weak signals—subtle indicators of future trends or risks—and highlights fascinating observations such as the increase in demand for rage rooms, the unexpected sale of Allbirds, and Waymo's pothole data sharing initiative. Estelle encourages readers to explore the future's possibilities through curiosity and engagement with emerging technologies, particularly AI in the corporate boardroom. The issue concludes with a call for feedback and an invitation to subscribe to continue receiving insights.
