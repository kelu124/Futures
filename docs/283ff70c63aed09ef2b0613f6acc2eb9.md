## Summary

Emad Mostaque, CEO of Stability AI, stated at the Goldman Sachs 2023 Disruptive Technology Symposium that AI will be more disruptive than the COVID-19 pandemic. He highlighted AI's rapid advancements, including its significant role during the pandemic and its impact on various sectors such as entertainment, education, and medicine. Mostaque noted that AI-generated software code now constitutes 41% of new code on GitHub and predicted that personalized AI tutoring could revolutionize education. He acknowledged potential job losses in programming and emphasized the swift changes AI is bringing to industries, warning that the pace of disruption is alarming.
