## Summary

Organizations are increasingly leveraging data, including alternative data sources, to gain insights that drive business outcomes, particularly in relation to economic and environmental factors. The advancement of technologies such as AI and computer vision applied to satellite data is enabling better tracking of human activity and its environmental impact. Different satellite types, including Earth-observation and atmospheric satellites, provide valuable data for macroeconomic indicators and ESG reporting. Robust model construction is vital for accurate insights, which can help close data gaps in ESG ratings and reveal the true environmental and economic conditions. Ultimately, satellite data is proving crucial for understanding the interconnectedness of economies and ecosystems, offering real-time insights for investors and organizations.
