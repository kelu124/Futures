## Summary

Cybersecurity is increasingly critical due to threats from hackers, notably Iranian-affiliated groups targeting U.S. infrastructure. Key sectors like energy, water, and healthcare are advised to bolster defenses, especially in light of breaches that expose vulnerabilities. Countries such as Germany and the UK face growing cyberattack risks, with infrastructure under strain from climate change and outdated systems. The automotive industry experiences breaches affecting electric vehicle data. Emerging technologies, including AI, complicate the cybersecurity landscape, alongside economic pressures affecting the workforce. The interconnectedness of global risks, including geopolitical instability and climate change, necessitates a holistic risk management approach, while the maritime industry faces new targeting by cybercriminals for financial gain.
