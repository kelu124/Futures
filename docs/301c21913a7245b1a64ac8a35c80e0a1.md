## Summary

Christopher Payne is an industrial photographer known for his meticulous work documenting manufacturing processes and facilities, including the MTA's repair shop. His photography captures both the technical and emotional aspects of production, showcasing the human effort behind the creation of everyday objects. Throughout his career, Payne has built relationships with various factories, gaining unique access to their inner workings. His recent project focuses on the MTA, reflecting a shift from documenting iconic subway cars to exploring the broader context of subway maintenance.
