## Summary

The article discusses the potential redundancy of Agile Coaches and Scrum Masters due to advances in artificial intelligence, specifically ChatGPT. The author shares experiences from three complex coaching scenarios run with ChatGPT, arguing that for routine tasks, AI could provide equivalent value to human coaches. While acknowledging that highly skilled coaches will still be necessary, the piece suggests that many organizations might rely on AI for standard coaching needs. The author emphasizes the importance of human coaches focusing on areas where they can add unique value, such as advanced coaching and leadership development, rather than routine tasks. The conclusion posits that AI could significantly change the landscape of Agile coaching within two years, urging professionals to adapt and differentiate themselves from AI capabilities.
