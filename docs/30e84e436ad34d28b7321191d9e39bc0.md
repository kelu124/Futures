## Summary

The author reflects on their long-standing insomnia and how they have come to embrace the night rather than fear it. After years of battling sleeplessness, particularly following personal losses, they explore the creative potential and unique mindset that comes with being awake at night. Citing historical and contemporary female writers and artists who have found inspiration in their sleepless nights, the author discusses how darkness alters thoughts and emotions, leading to heightened creativity. They also touch on the physiological changes that occur at night, the societal perceptions of insomnia, and their own journey toward appreciating the gifts of darkness, ultimately transforming their relationship with sleep and creativity.
