## Summary

OpenAI has announced it will begin testing advertisements in the ChatGPT app for certain US users, marking a significant shift from CEO Sam Altman's earlier stance against ads. The banner ads will target free and ChatGPT Go users, which recently launched globally. However, subscribers to higher-tier plans will remain ad-free. The ads will be integrated at the bottom of answers when relevant products or services are available, clearly labeled to distinguish them from regular responses. This move follows OpenAI's previous introduction of shopping features and aligns with trends of other AI companies exploring ad revenues, like Google.
