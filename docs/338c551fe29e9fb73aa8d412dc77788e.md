## Summary

Rishad Tobaccowala reflects on the evolution of technology and its impact on talent, emphasizing that while technology serves as leverage, it is ultimately human skill that drives success. He recalls his educational journey, particularly the significance of leverage in Mechanics and how it parallels advancements in technology. Tobaccowala highlights the importance of embracing technology, continuous learning, and the combination of digital and analog approaches. He outlines five strategies for thriving in the AI age: embracing technology, investing in learning, recognizing the importance of human talent, and honing one's skills. The central message is that while technology can amplify capabilities, it is the talent that truly makes a difference.
