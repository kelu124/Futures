## Summary

The text discusses the concept of 'Every Single X' projects, where individuals set out to experience every instance of a particular category, such as visiting every bookstore or public park in a city. Inspired by anecdotes from various contributors, the author highlights the joy and adventure of completing such quests, even if they are open-ended or destined to 'fail' due to changing circumstances. The text also introduces Substack's new 'Notes' feature for sharing thoughts and experiences. The author encourages readers to create their own 'Every Single X' projects, emphasizing the personal rewards of such endeavors.
