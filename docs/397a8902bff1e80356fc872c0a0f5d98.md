## Summary

This article highlights seven unique projects awarded microgrants that aim to cultivate a future centered on love. The selected initiatives, emerging from over 80 global proposals, focus on community healing, food security, intergenerational connections, and economic reimagination. Projects include transforming a fishing vessel into a space for grieving in Italy, reviving rice farming traditions in Japan, creating a recipe-based cultural exchange in Milan, and fostering creative kinship among children. Others explore online connections through shared vulnerability and compile generational wisdom on authentic relationships. The organization emphasizes love as a shared responsibility and encourages community involvement in these transformative projects.
