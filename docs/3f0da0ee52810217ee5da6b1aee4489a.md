## Summary

The horror genre has surged in popularity post-pandemic, capturing a 13% market share of the box office in 2021, surpassing comedies, dramas, and thrillers for the first time since 1995. This growth is attributed to viewers seeking comfort through horror, as it allows them to confront fears safely, explore dark curiosities, and find resolution in uncertain times. The genre's appeal extends to various formats, facilitating community participation and creativity on platforms like Reddit. The supernatural offers explanations for the inexplicable, providing a sense of control amidst chaos. Despite its unsettling themes, horror ultimately serves as a therapeutic outlet, fostering adaptability and shared understanding in a world marked by ambiguity and anxiety.
