## Summary

Governments globally have sought to enhance efficiency and productivity through various reforms, reflecting private sector innovations. Historical efforts include the adoption of technology from the telegraph to digital transformation, with initiatives like the Government Performance and Results Act in the U.S. and similar frameworks worldwide. Recent advancements in artificial intelligence (AI) have further propelled this transformation, enabling improved operations like the IRS's automation of tax return processing. Governments are now leveraging AI and digital tools to drive productivity, streamline services, and enhance citizen engagement. However, the challenge remains in balancing efficiency with equity in service delivery. As AI continues to evolve, it presents opportunities for deeper productivity improvements and requires enhanced data literacy among public sector employees to fully utilize these technologies.
