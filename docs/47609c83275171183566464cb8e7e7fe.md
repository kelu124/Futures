## Summary

The emergence of AI, particularly since the launch of ChatGPT in November 2022, has sparked discussions about its impact on jobs. While many CEOs express optimism about AI automating tedious tasks and creating new job opportunities, Klarna CEO Sebastian Siemiatkowski provides a contrary perspective, predicting significant job losses as AI's capabilities grow. He highlights Klarna's cost-saving measures by replacing human roles with AI, raising concerns about career obsolescence, especially for middle-skilled jobs. As companies brace for an AI-driven future, the discussion reveals skepticism around the accompanying investment bubble and implications for job security. The article emphasizes the importance of preparing for the transformative effects of AI on society and the workforce, advocating reflection on its broader consequences for humanity.
