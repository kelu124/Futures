## Summary

The article discusses the surprising capabilities of artificial intelligence (AI), illustrated through Google's Project Loon, which controlled balloons that unexpectedly learned the ancient sailing technique of "tacking". This incident highlights AI's ability to develop novel strategies independently, sometimes leading to unpredictable outcomes. The text references various AI experiments, including DeepMind's AlphaGo and OpenAI's GPT-2, showcasing AI's potential for creative problem-solving. However, experts caution about the unpredictability of AI behavior and the implications for safety in real-world applications. The exploration nature of AI can yield both innovative solutions and unexpected risks, underscoring the need for transparency in AI development.
