## Summary

This article discusses how to effectively onboard and work with AI as an 'intern.' It emphasizes treating AI as a collaborative entity rather than just software, highlighting the importance of understanding its strengths and weaknesses to maximize productivity. Readers are encouraged to categorize tasks into 'Just Me,' 'Delegated,' 'Centaur,' and 'Automated' tasks to determine how best to utilize AI. The author suggests developing a clear job description for the AI, experimenting with its capabilities, and maintaining oversight. As AI technology evolves, users are urged to adapt and consider future possibilities while recognizing the current limitations of AI. The article concludes by encouraging a mindset shift towards viewing AI as a valuable team member rather than a mere tool.
