## Summary

Red Bull has introduced a groundbreaking magazine cover that doubles as a playable game system, featuring Tetris. Designed by Kevin Bates, the GamePop GP-1 consists of a flexible circuit board integrated with 180 RGB LEDs and capacitive touch buttons, all within a magazine cover that feels like traditional print yet offers interactive gaming. It highlights innovative engineering, with a focus on making technology unobtrusive. Though limited in functionality, the GP-1 serves as a collector's item and a celebration of print's potential when reimagined creatively. Red Bull's approach emphasizes that print is not dying but evolving through bold design and creativity.
