## Summary

The article highlights the significant opportunity in B2B marketplaces, estimating that $100 trillion flows between businesses annually, compared to $3.5 trillion in consumer spending. While B2C marketplaces like Uber and Airbnb have dominated innovation, B2B markets remain largely untapped, with only 5-10% of transactions occurring online. The piece uses Faire, a successful wholesale marketplace connecting independent retailers with brands, as a case study. Faire leverages technology to enhance discovery and reduce inefficiencies, allowing retailers to compete against giants like Amazon. The rise of vertical B2B marketplaces, tailored to specific sectors, is noted as a growing trend. The author anticipates a surge in B2B marketplace startups due to improved technology and changing workforce expectations, suggesting the 2020s will be pivotal for this sector.
