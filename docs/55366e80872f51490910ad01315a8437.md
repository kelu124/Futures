## Summary

Google Maps has introduced eco-friendly routing features across nearly 40 European countries, allowing users to choose routes that optimize for lower fuel consumption, thereby saving money and reducing carbon emissions. The app displays both the fastest and the most fuel-efficient routes, helping users make informed decisions. Additionally, drivers can select their engine type to receive tailored routing based on vehicle efficiency. Google Maps also supports sustainable travel options like walking, cycling, and public transport, providing detailed route information and real-time updates. The initiative aims to empower users to make sustainable choices and is part of Google's broader commitment to promote eco-friendly practices.
