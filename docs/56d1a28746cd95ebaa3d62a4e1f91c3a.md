## Summary

The text discusses the threats to democracy posed by various malicious social media tactics, including misinformation, manipulation, and exploitation of cognitive biases. These tactics undermine informed public discourse and democratic engagement. To combat these threats, the report suggests developing immunity activators for healthy cognition, such as using public media platforms, establishing data ownership rules, creating tech solutions against digital deception, implementing early warning systems, promoting positive online behaviors, enhancing media literacy, and forming independent platform review bodies to ensure transparency.
