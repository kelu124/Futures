## Summary

The article explores the challenges facing women in the workplace, highlighting systemic gender biases and the pressures to conform to societal norms. Tressie McMillan Cottom criticizes the advice given by influencers who promote unrealistic expectations for women, such as embracing AI for career advancement. She argues that the current labor market, influenced by economic changes and corporate resistance to diversity, dismantles the traditional paths for female success. The discussion also touches on the political landscape, where women are often blamed for societal issues like declining birth rates. Ultimately, Cottom emphasizes the need to rethink support systems for women rather than adhering to outdated empowerment platitudes.
