## Summary

The article emphasizes the important role of science fiction in fostering inclusive conversations about science and technology policy. It presents science fiction as a valuable tool for exploring potential futures by engaging the audience's imagination through storytelling. By centering human experiences in speculative narratives, science fiction enables individuals to empathize with future scenarios and consider the broader implications of technological advancements. The author advocates for collaborative workshops, where diverse experts can imagine and articulate positive futures, particularly in areas like climate change and AI. Ultimately, the piece calls for a shift from incrementalism to transformative thinking, encouraging more people to engage in discussions about the future and to reimagine societal structures for a better tomorrow.
