## Summary

The text describes a photograph of the Leaf Sheep Nudibranch (Costasiella kuroshimae) taken by Diego Delso in Anilao, Philippines, on August 23, 2023. The nudibranch measures 5–10 millimeters in length, and the image was captured using a 100 mm macro lens with a diopter for magnification. The photograph is part of the Wiki Science Competition 2023 and is licensed under CC BY-SA, allowing for legal sharing and remixing with attribution.
