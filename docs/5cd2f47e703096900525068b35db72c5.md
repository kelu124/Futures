## Summary

Google Japan has introduced the Gboard Double-Sided Version, a unique Möbius strip keyboard, as part of its annual quirky keyboard tradition. This keyboard weighs 20.8 donuts and features a 360-degree design with 208 keys, doubling the typical key count found in standard keyboards. While it's not commercially available, users can download schematics and firmware from GitHub to create their own. The keyboard supports both English and Japanese and boasts customizable RGB lighting with over 16 million colors. Made from 26 modules, it offers Cherry MX-compatible hot-swappable switches. Though unlikely to become a mainstream choice, its eccentric design and potential as a gaming device make it an intriguing project for tech enthusiasts.
