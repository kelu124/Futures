## Summary

The text explores the concept of technological Singularity, a point where advancements in technology, particularly artificial intelligence (AI), could fundamentally change human life. It reflects on how past generations couldn't foresee the emergence of social networks, search engines, and AI-driven navigation tools. The author discusses predictions by thinkers like Vernor Vinge and Ray Kurzweil, who foresee a future dominated by superintelligent AI, potentially leading to an intelligence explosion. While some scientists express concern about AI's risks, others highlight its potential to enhance human capabilities. The text concludes by emphasizing the importance of collaboration between humans and AI, suggesting that embracing this partnership is essential for future success.
