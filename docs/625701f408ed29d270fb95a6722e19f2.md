## Summary

Germany is enhancing its aerial surveillance of Russian submarines, with Defense Minister Boris Pistorius participating in a reconnaissance flight using a Boeing P-8 Poseidon from Lossiemouth, Scotland. The flight aimed to monitor submarine activities in the North Atlantic, amid growing concerns over increased Russian submarine movements. The German Navy is acquiring eight Poseidon aircraft to join NATO allies in surveillance efforts. This initiative highlights a commitment to European security and collaboration, including support for Ukraine. The ministers emphasized the importance of NATO in deterring Russian threats and maintaining regional security, alongside the expansion of military cooperation between Germany and the UK.
