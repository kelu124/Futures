## Summary

In a recent Reddit AMA, OpenAI CEO Sam Altman and other officials discussed the company's challenges, including competition from Chinese firms like DeepSeek. Altman acknowledged the need for a revised open-source strategy due to diminishing lead in AI capabilities. The team also revealed ongoing plans for a significant data center project, aimed at meeting increasing compute demands. There was emphasis on transparency in model reasoning to improve user experience. Despite rumors, Altman expressed hopes of making ChatGPT cheaper over time. OpenAI is partnering with the U.S. government for nuclear defense research and continues to explore advancements in AI models, including future releases of reasoning and image-generation models.
