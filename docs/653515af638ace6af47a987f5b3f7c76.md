## Summary

A single-page website, diskprices.com, created by Jeremy Grosser, generates $5,000 a month through affiliate marketing despite its simple design. The site lists discounted hard drive prices from Amazon and allows users to filter by various criteria. It serves as a valuable resource for those seeking cost-effective storage solutions. The success of diskprices demonstrates that a modern, complex website isn't necessary to earn significant income online, as the site continuously updates its listings and operates efficiently with minimal costs.
