## Summary

A new AI chatbot called "gpt2-chatbot" has recently become available on a language model benchmarking site. This chatbot is generating a lot of attention and is believed to have similar capabilities as OpenAI's GPT-4. The origins of the gpt2-chatbot are unclear, but it has impressed users with its ability to code a perfect clone of Flappy Bird and solve a math problem from the International Math Olympiad. Speculation about its origins ranges from OpenAI to Google or Anthropic, but there is no concrete evidence. The gpt2-chatbot can be tested on the LMSYS Org website, although it is currently an anonymous model available for testing purposes only. The capabilities of the gpt2-chatbot have been compared to other leading AI models, and it has shown advanced reasoning and problem-solving abilities. While there is limited information available, it is expected that more details about the creator and origins of gpt2-chatbot will be revealed in the future.
