## Summary

Swedish perfume brand Koyia has opened a unique shop in the Sm√•land woods where customers pay for fragrances with time spent in nature rather than money. Specifically, they exchange 599 seconds of silence for the perfume, reflecting research that suggests this duration fosters positive health benefits. Koyia's alcohol-free product incorporates natural phytoncides aimed at boosting immunity and reducing stress. This innovative payment model challenges conventional economic values by prioritizing human experiences and well-being over profit. The initiative redefines worth from monetary to personal transformation and loyalty, positioning the brand as a cultural leader advocating for the significance of time spent in nature.
