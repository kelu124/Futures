## Summary

Radha Agrawal, an entrepreneur driven by a lifelong desire for belonging, has founded various initiatives to combat loneliness, the latest being Belong Center. This nonprofit aims to empower belonging through communal experiences and guided conversations, termed Belong Circles. Agrawal's journey includes launching Daybreaker, a sober dance party, and co-founding Thinx. Inspired by her research on community and loneliness, she has organized unique experiences like excursions to Antarctica and Egypt, gathering influential leaders to raise awareness and funds for social causes. Despite challenges, Agrawal believes in the potential of collective experiences to foster genuine connections and ultimately eradicate loneliness, planning numerous initiatives to facilitate this vision.
