## Summary

This article presents 26 predictions for 2026 regarding technology and media convergence, categorized into two parts. It highlights the likely emergence of advertisements in ChatGPT to monetize its large user base, the potential IPO of Anthropic contrasting Stripe's decision to remain private, and the increasing trend of synthetic media in platforms like Instagram and TikTok. Moreover, it discusses expected upheavals in the music industry, ticketing sector, and Hollywood related to AI, and anticipates a boom in generative media and AI-enabled services. The article concludes with predictions about pricing models shifting in SaaS and how intra-agent interactions could streamline operations. Overall, it outlines a transformative year ahead for technology, media, and business models.
