## Summary

The text discusses a shift in technology from products designed to capture attention to those that give it back, emphasizing the emergence of simple and powerful AI-driven tools. These products aim to operate in the background, freeing users from constant oversight and allowing them to focus on more complex tasks. The author references the concept of 'calm technology,' advocating for systems that enhance human capability rather than demand attention. With AI managing mundane tasks invisibly, users can allocate their mental energy to higher-order challenges. This evolving landscape promises a future where tools are seamless and unobtrusive, ultimately leading to improved productivity and reduced stress for users.
