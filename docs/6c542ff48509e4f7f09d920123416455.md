## Summary

The construction industry is poised for transformation by 2025, driven by emerging technologies and data-driven solutions. Key trends identified by Trimble include the rise of AI and Machine Learning, which enhance efficiency and decision-making, and the importance of seamless technology integration to improve data interoperability across various platforms. Advances in Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) technology will be crucial for precise positioning, while improved data management for mixed fleets will enhance operational efficiency. Safety on construction sites will benefit from innovative tools and reality capture technologies. Additionally, the industry faces a labor shortage, which automation can help mitigate. Subscription-based models for technology access are on the rise, allowing contractors to maximize resources. As robotics and AI-driven design tools evolve, the construction landscape is set for a smarter, safer future, offering significant competitive advantages to those who adapt.
