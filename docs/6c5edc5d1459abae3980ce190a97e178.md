## Summary

US Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) is seeking information from companies about commercial Big Data and Ad Tech products to aid in investigative activities. This request, published in the Federal Register, aims to identify tools that can help manage the rising volume of information ICE encounters from various sources. Although details are sparse regarding specific regulations or privacy standards, the request highlights a growing interest in using technologies initially created for digital advertising for government surveillance and law enforcement. ICE has previously acquired services for mobile location data from different companies, using it to enhance its investigative capabilities. This development occurs against a backdrop of heightened federal scrutiny related to immigration enforcement actions and incidents involving federal officers.
