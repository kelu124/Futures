## Summary

A usage study by OpenAI revealed that nearly 15% of work-related conversations on ChatGPT relate to decision-making. Maj. Gen. William "Hank" Taylor of the U.S. military disclosed that he is utilizing AI chatbots to modernize predictive analysis for logistics and operational planning. During the Association of the U.S. Army Conference, he emphasized that these tools assist in decision-making at both individual and organizational levels. While the use of AI in the military raises concerns about its reliability and accuracy, it is being employed to enhance processes like report writing and strategic decision-making, marking a significant development in military practices.
