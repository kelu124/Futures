## Summary

The text predicts that while 2025 has been disastrous, 2026 may be less severe but still challenging politically and socially. It anticipates infighting among Trump's supporters and growing division within the Republican Party. The Democrats face their own internal struggles between populist factions. AI technology advancements will continue, with significant public backlash against its implications, particularly in finance. The looming issues include mass media control by billionaires, potential wars, public health crises, and more environmental damage. Despite the predictive somber outlook, there is a hint of optimism for recovery as problems are acknowledged.
