## Summary

Recent concerns have emerged regarding the misuse of AI chatbots like ChatGPT in producing scientific literature, leading to questionable integrity in published papers. Researchers have noted suspicious phrases typical of AI-generated text appearing in academic works, which poses risks of inaccuracies and fabricated references. Studies indicate a significant rise in AI-generated language across scientific articles, with some papers showing clear signs of AI involvement. The use of AI tools, while potentially helpful for language assistance, raises alarms about the quality and reliability of scientific writing and the peer-review process. Experts warn that reliance on AI could lead to flawed research outcomes, emphasizing the need for caution in integrating these technologies into scientific practices.
