## Summary

Poland is launching its "Always Prepared" military training program to offer military training to all citizens, aiming to train 400,000 people by 2026. This initiative, described as the largest defense training in Polish history, is a response to heightened security concerns following Russia's invasion of Ukraine. The training will include courses on basic security, survival skills, medical training, and cyber hygiene. Poland's Defense Minister emphasized the importance of preparedness, citing the experiences of Ukraine during the invasion. The program aims to strengthen the military reserves and enhance community resilience in light of current geopolitical tensions.
