## Summary

This text presents an extensive guide to AI image synthesis tools and resources for prompt designers. It covers various models such as Dalle2, MidJourney, and StableDiffusion, categorizing them into commercial, free hosted, and free local options. It also includes tools for prompt engineering, post-processing, and community resources, along with tutorials and learning materials. The guide encourages contributions and highlights upcoming projects in the field, making it a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in AI-generated art.
