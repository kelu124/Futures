## Summary

In a rapidly changing world characterized by chaos and uncertainty, the importance of futurism and foresight is paramount. Sean Moffitt, co-founder of Grey Swan Guild, outlines 20 compelling reasons why these disciplines help individuals and organizations navigate the complexities of modern life. Key insights emphasize staying ahead of accelerated change, avoiding blind spots, and harnessing knowledge to seize opportunities. Foresight allows for better decision-making amid unpredictability, fostering resilience and adaptability. It promotes a long-term perspective essential for sustainable success, encourages collaborative problem-solving, and drives social innovation. Ultimately, embracing futurism equips people to envision and create a hopeful future despite challenges.
