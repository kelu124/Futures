## Summary

The tech sector is responsible for nearly 3% of global emissions, prompting companies to evaluate their environmental impact and adopt sustainable practices. Western Digital emphasizes the importance of integrating sustainability into company strategies, which can enhance employee engagement, customer satisfaction, and investor interest. The company encourages engineers to prioritize sustainability in product design and operations, with a focus on reducing waste and utilizing renewable resources. Western Digital has set ambitious goals, including 100% renewable energy and net zero emissions by 2032. Tracking progress is crucial, as companies risk losing credibility if they fail to meet sustainability targets. Ultimately, fostering trust and transparency in sustainability commitments is key to maintaining company value and reputation.
