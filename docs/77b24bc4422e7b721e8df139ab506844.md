## Summary

In the 296th edition of his thought letter, Rishad introduces guest posts and emphasizes the importance of being human in the age of AI. Guest contributor David Armano shares insights on shifting the focus from being the smartest person to the most human person in the room. He highlights the increasing commodification of knowledge and the rise of 'Relationship Work,' which values empathy and genuine human connections over mere information processing. Armano advocates for using AI tools while enhancing human presence and orchestration in professional settings. He concludes by recommending a short film, "Retirement Plan," to inspire readers to appreciate life's temporary nature, advocating for a deeper understanding of what it means to live fully and humanly.
