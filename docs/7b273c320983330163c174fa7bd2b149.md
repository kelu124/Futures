## Summary

Jamelle Bouie emphasizes the unpredictable consequences of actions in technology and advocates for cautious decision-making. In the realm of AI, major companies like Amazon, Google, Microsoft, and Meta reported record capital spending of $130.65 billion in early 2023 to enhance AI infrastructure, significantly outpacing historical military spending. However, while investments soar, issues of financial management and tax policies are highlighted. In California, regulations allow police to ticket driverless cars, signaling increasing governance over autonomous vehicles. Meanwhile, India faces AI-induced layoffs, particularly in its once-thriving service sector, as giant companies like Tata Consultancy Services cut jobs, forcing new graduates to adapt their skills amidst a contracting job market for tech-driven roles.
