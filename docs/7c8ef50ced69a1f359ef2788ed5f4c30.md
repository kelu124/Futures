## Summary

The playbook "Fungi as your Futurist" presents innovative approaches to tackling humanity's challenges by drawing inspiration from fungi. It outlines 12 regenerative design principles aimed at fostering symbiotic futures across various sectors. The project is rooted in multidisciplinary insights from experts in mycology, design, and foresight, and emphasizes the importance of adopting systems-thinking. The toolkit is designed for organizations focused on sustainability and innovation, providing exercises and practical tools for implementing fungal thinking. The initiative, led by Sporesight's Jess Jorgensen, is informed by global collaborations and qualitative research exploring the potential of fungi in transforming design and business practices towards more regenerative models. It encourages imaginative thinking to shift from extraction to regeneration, inviting everyone to cultivate deeper relationships with nature.
