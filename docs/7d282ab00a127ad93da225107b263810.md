## Summary

At London Tech Week, U.K. Prime Minister Rishi Sunak announced that Google DeepMind, OpenAI, and Anthropic will provide their AI models to the U.K. government for research and safety purposes. This initiative aims to better evaluate the opportunities and risks associated with AI, with Sunak emphasizing the need for safe AI development. He highlighted the U.K.'s ambition to lead in global AI safety regulation and announced a forthcoming global summit on AI safety, akin to climate change conferences. Additionally, a Foundation Model Taskforce will be established, supported by £100 million in funding, to advance AI safety research.
