## Summary

The text explores the significance of democratizing the imagination of the future, emphasizing that collective dreaming can lead to a more equitable world. It reflects on insights gained from years of supporting various futuring practitioners, such as artists and social workers, who are broadening the scope of who can envision the future. The report discusses the importance of nurturing a positive relationship with the future, the healing potential of imaginative engagement, and how envisioning a thriving future reveals present inequalities. Ultimately, it calls for a love for the future that inspires meaningful action today. The report encourages learning from practitioners and staying updated on futures thinking.
