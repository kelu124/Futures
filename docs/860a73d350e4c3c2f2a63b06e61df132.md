## Summary

The Metaverse Standards Forum, announced on June 21, 2022, aims to promote open standards essential for building an interoperable metaverse. Membership is free and open to any organization, with founding members including major companies like Adobe, Microsoft, and Epic Games. The Forum seeks to address interoperability challenges hindering metaverse deployment and will focus on practical projects such as prototyping, hackathons, and development of guidelines. With the participation of numerous standards organizations, the Forum's goal is to foster collaboration and expedite the creation of these standards, ensuring a cohesive metaverse experience. Industry leaders emphasize that an open metaverse relies on shared standards to facilitate innovation and inclusivity.
