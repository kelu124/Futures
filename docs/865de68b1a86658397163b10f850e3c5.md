## Summary

Ben, a Toronto-based professional, discovered the trend of 'overemployment' where individuals secretly hold multiple jobs, largely fueled by advancements in AI like ChatGPT. Initially skeptical, he began using ChatGPT to streamline his work in marketing, eventually taking on a second job with the help of the AI. Many in this community leverage ChatGPT for various tasks, from coding to writing, allowing them to manage multiple jobs efficiently. While this creates opportunities for increased income, it raises concerns about job automation. Despite understanding the risks, these workers feel empowered by AI to optimize their workload, with some even dreaming of further automation to maximize their earnings. However, figures like Ryan express unease about the long-term implications of AI on job stability, likening it to the industrial revolution's impact on traditional labor.
