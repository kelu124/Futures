## Summary

Sort A Bricks offers a unique service where customers can send their mixed LEGO® bricks to be sorted and restored into complete, ready-to-build sets using AI technology. Customers pack their bricks and ship them to the sorting facility, where they will receive a list of potential sets that can be restored. The process involves brick analysis, optional sourcing of missing pieces, and sorting into complete sets. The service is designed to clean and organize bricks while making it easy for users to enjoy their LEGO® collection again. Sort A Bricks aims to make brick reuse fun, affordable, and sustainable, targeting customers in Western Europe with plans for global expansion.
