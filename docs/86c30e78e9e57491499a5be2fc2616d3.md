## Summary

The article discusses the innovative use of AI to create aesthetically pleasing yet functional invisible QR codes, merging art and technology. With tools like ControlNet and Stable Diffusion, designers can embed QR codes into images seamlessly, ensuring they remain scannable while enhancing visual appeal. The process involves generating QR codes, selecting images, and integrating them without compromising functionality. The article emphasizes the importance of balancing aesthetics with the core purpose of QR codes and explores future possibilities of AI in design, including customizable interfaces and immersive experiences, marking the beginning of a new era in digital design.
