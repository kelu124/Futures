## Summary

Kyle Newman, director of Fanboys, coauthored "Lore and Legends: A Visual Celebration of the World’s Greatest Roleplaying Game," focusing on the resurgence of Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) since its 2014 5th Edition launch. The book discusses the game's rise from near extinction to 60 million players, influenced by '80s nostalgia, geek culture, and digital technology. Celebrities like Dwayne Johnson and Steven Colbert have embraced D&D, contributing to its popularity. Newman is also working on a D&D documentary for its 50th anniversary in 2024, highlighting the game's community and legacy. He shares insights on various D&D projects and the success of the recent D&D film, emphasizing that timing and marketing impact its reception.
