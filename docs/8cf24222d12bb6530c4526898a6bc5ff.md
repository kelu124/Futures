## Summary

Enola Holmes 2, directed by Harry Bradbeer and released on Netflix in November 2022, is a sequel to the 2020 film featuring Millie Bobby Brown as Enola Holmes, the teenage sister of Sherlock Holmes. The film follows Enola as she establishes her own detective agency but struggles to find clients. Her investigation into the disappearance of a factory worker's sister leads her to uncover a conspiracy involving a deadly epidemic and exploitation of workers at a match factory. The plot intertwines with Sherlock's case of government blackmail, culminating in a dramatic confrontation. The film received positive reviews, praised for its engaging narrative and performances, particularly from Brown and Henry Cavill, and topped Netflix's viewership charts in 93 countries.
