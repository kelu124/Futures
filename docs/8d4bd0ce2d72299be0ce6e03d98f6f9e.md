## Summary

The text discusses the concept of choosing between 'alive time' and 'dead time' in life, emphasizing the importance of being proactive and making the most of every moment, even in challenging situations. The author shares personal experiences from a frustrating consulting job and references advice from Robert Greene about utilizing time effectively. Through examples like Malcolm X, who transformed his prison sentence into a period of intense learning, the narrative encourages readers to resist complacency and find productive ways to engage with their circumstances. Ultimately, it asserts that the choice is ours: to embrace alive time, filled with growth and learning, or to let time pass without purpose.
