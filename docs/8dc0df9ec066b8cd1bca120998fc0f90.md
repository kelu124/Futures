## Summary

The OSPOS for Good 2024 event at the UN showcased the growing significance of Open Source in the realm of sustainable development. Key speakers emphasized that Open Source is integral to addressing global issues, despite concerns regarding its evolving nature and the challenges posed by artificial intelligence. Discussions revealed a disconnect between formal protocols and the tech community's culture, highlighting the need for more engaging formats beyond traditional panels. The event also underscored the importance of recognizing Open Source as a critical infrastructure, requiring ongoing maintenance and community support. Participants were encouraged to integrate Open Source solutions into their initiatives for achieving Sustainable Development Goals, marking the beginning of a collaborative effort within the UN framework.
