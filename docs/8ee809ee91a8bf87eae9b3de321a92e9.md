## Summary

The article discusses the concept of luggage-free travel as a potential future trend in the tourism industry, driven by sustainable practices and changing perceptions of packing. Various destinations, such as Japan and Dubrovnik, are encouraging travelers to pack lighter or rent clothing to reduce their carbon footprints and alleviate overcrowding in popular tourist areas. While some industry leaders embrace these changes, there are concerns about shifting responsibilities from airlines to individual travelers. Ultimately, the focus on minimizing luggage pairs with the broader goal of enhancing sustainability in travel.
