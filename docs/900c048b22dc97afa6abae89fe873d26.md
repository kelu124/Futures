## Summary

J.Lo's AI thriller, 'Atlas,' may not have received positive reviews from film critics, but it has become immensely popular on Netflix with over 60 million views in its first two weeks. The film explores the themes of artificial intelligence and technology, shedding light on pro-progress ideas that are often overlooked in popular culture. While it may not be an award-winning film, Atlas captures the attention of sci-fi enthusiasts and entertains them with its story of an elite counterterrorism analyst teaming up with an artificially intelligent mech-suit to stop an evil AI robot from destroying humanity.
