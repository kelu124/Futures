## Summary

J.Lo's AI thriller 'Atlas' on Netflix, despite poor critical reception, has gained significant popularity, attracting over 60 million views in its first two weeks. While it may not be acclaimed for its cinematic quality, the film presents important pro-progress ideas about artificial intelligence. It features J.Lo as a counterterrorism analyst teaming up with an AI mech-suit to combat an evil AI robot. The film's widespread viewership exposes millions to fundamental concepts about AI and technology, which are often overlooked in mainstream media.
