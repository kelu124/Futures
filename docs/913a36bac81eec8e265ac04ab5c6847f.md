## Summary

Mothers, feeling overwhelmed by balancing full-time work and parenting, are increasingly turning to AI tools like ChatGPT for support. Lilian Schmidt, a mother of two, uses ChatGPT for organizational tasks, meal planning, and even bedtime routines, finding it helpful in reducing stress and allowing for more quality time with her children. She highlights the challenges faced by parents in managing multiple responsibilities while maintaining a calm home environment. Experts like Lorain Moorehead emphasize that AI can help streamline parenting logistics and free up mental space for engagement with children. The use of AI is seen as a valuable resource for real-time adjustments, helping parents focus on being present rather than getting bogged down by daily tasks.
