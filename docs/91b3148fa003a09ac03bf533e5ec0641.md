## Summary

On May 12th, a unique event called BassBath was held in Shanghai to promote inclusivity in nightlife, specifically for the deaf community at the techno club Heim. Organized by Transparent Afternoon members, the event featured interactive games led by deaf artist Alice Hu and a performance by deaf rapper Xumin, who also taught basic sign language. A professional interpreter ensured smooth communication. This event is part of a growing trend of deaf-friendly nightlife experiences in China, with plans for BassBath to continue as a series of events that foster connections between the deaf and hearing communities. Participants are already forming friendships and sharing updates through a dedicated chat group.
