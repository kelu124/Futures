## Summary

David Balland, a French cryptocurrency executive and co-founder of Ledger, was kidnapped from his home on January 21, 2025. The kidnappers demanded cryptocurrency payments and inflicted severe violence, including mutilation, during the ordeal. After nearly 24 hours, the French National Gendarmerie conducted a tactical rescue operation, freeing Balland and his wife without casualties. The incident has raised alarm in the cryptocurrency industry, especially as Bitcoin prices surge to record highs. The ongoing investigation aims to apprehend the kidnappers, highlighting the increasing dangers faced by cryptocurrency executives amidst rising crime in the sector.
