## Summary

The article discusses the transformative impact of generative AI, particularly ChatGPT, on productivity across various industries. Initially skeptical about technological disruptions, the author highlights recent studies indicating significant productivity gainsâ€”up to 55.8% for programmers and 37% faster task completion for other professionals using AI tools. The findings suggest that AI's influence extends beyond low-skilled jobs to highly skilled professions such as academics and lawyers, challenging the notion that automation will primarily affect manual labor. The article warns of a potential reluctance among workers to disclose their use of AI, creating a landscape of 'secret cyborgs.' Ultimately, it emphasizes the urgent need for individuals and organizations to embrace AI tools to improve performance and adapt to the ongoing disruption in the workplace.
