## Summary

The article discusses the rise of digitally-native jobs and how technology companies are creating ecosystems that facilitate job creation beyond their own employment numbers. Walmart and Amazon are highlighted as major employers, with Amazon's ecosystem allowing millions of sellers to earn income through its platform. The piece explores new job titles that have emerged in the digital age, such as Discord Community Manager, Roblox Developer, and Etsy Seller. It provides examples of individuals like Miss Excel, KARRA, and Bella McFadden, who have built successful careers using online platforms. The article emphasizes the internet's role in democratizing entrepreneurship and creating diverse income opportunities, while also noting the potential for future ecosystems to enable millions to thrive in the digital economy.
