## Summary

The author shares various highlights, including an upcoming interview with Hrishikesh Hirway, reflections on crazy sock day, and the excitement surrounding their book, "Don’t Call It Art." They emphasize the importance of silly rituals in creative work and the joy of re-reading books like "Lonesome Dove," which offers new insights over time. The author also discusses the evolving perspectives on art, quoting Blake Gopnik's view of art as a verb. They offer a peek into comedian Sheng Wang's stand-up approach and promote a winter mixtape, "Snow in April," while paying tribute to singer Asha Bhosle. Readers are encouraged to support the author's work through book pre-orders.
