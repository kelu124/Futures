## Summary

The article discusses the growing popularity of short-form videos, particularly on platforms like TikTok and Instagram, driven by shrinking attention spans among consumers. Research indicates that the average attention span has decreased significantly, with people now typically focusing on content for around 47 seconds. Short-form videos, defined as lasting less than 60 seconds, are favored for their digestibility and ease of consumption. They also require less production effort and can effectively convey information quickly. Marketers recognize their value, planning to increase investments in this content type for higher returns. The convenience of short-form videos allows viewers to engage with content anytime, anywhere, making them a lasting trend in media consumption.
