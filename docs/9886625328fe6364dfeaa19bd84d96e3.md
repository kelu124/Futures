## Summary

Established companies increasingly recognize the need to innovate for growth in the digital era, leading to the creation of innovation labs and programs. However, many companies struggle with a disconnect between their innovation efforts and strategic priorities, resulting in a lack of meaningful growth despite numerous creative ideas. To bridge this gap, corporate innovation must be aligned with clear strategic goals and leverage the companyâ€™s strengths. The author suggests five steps to integrate strategy into the innovation process effectively.
