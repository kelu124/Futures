## Summary

Hasbro, the toy giant, takes a unique approach to leadership development by offering a two-day crash course on business fundamentals followed by an immersive role-playing game called Toy Tycoon. This game simulates the complexities of running a toy company, challenging participants to make critical decisions as they navigate various business scenarios. Hasbro CEO Chris Cocks believes this method helps potential leaders enhance their strategic thinking and teamwork skills while embodying the company's playful spirit. The idea is supported by other business leaders like Elon Musk and Reid Hoffman, who advocate for the strategic benefits of gaming in developing leadership qualities and decision-making skills.
