## Summary

Games Workshop has banned the use of AI in its content production and design process, with CEO Kevin Rountree noting that none of the company's senior managers are enthusiastic about the technology. While a few managers are permitted to experiment with AI, there is a strict internal policy against using AI-generated content or design processes. The company prefers to safeguard its intellectual property and respect human creators, continuing to invest in creative talent for its Warhammer Studio. This stance contrasts with other entertainment companies that are embracing AI, despite some community backlash regarding the authenticity of creative works.
