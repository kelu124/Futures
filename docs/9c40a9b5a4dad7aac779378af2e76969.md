## Summary

Power users of ChatGPT, referred to as 'sloppers', are criticized for over-reliance on AI for everyday tasks, as highlighted in recent viral discussions. This term, coined by a TikTok user, reflects a growing resentment towards AI technologies, pointing to concerns over low-quality content, and their potential to replace human jobs. Anecdotes illustrate the absurdity of dependence on AI in personal scenarios, raising alarm about the consequences of diminished critical thinking skills and the spread of misinformation. Commentators debate alternative terms for such users, emphasizing the cultural shift driven by AI. As reliance on AI escalates, critics warn of a 'slopper pandemic' diminishing human reasoning.
