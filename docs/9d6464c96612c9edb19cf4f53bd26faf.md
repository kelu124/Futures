# __Technology's Rapid Evolution Over Time__, from ([20241027](https://kghosh.substack.com/p/20241027).)

__[External link](https://ourworldindata.org/technology-long-run)__



## Summary

The text discusses how dramatically technology has changed over time, emphasizing that what seems unimaginable today could become common in the near future. It highlights the rapid pace of technological advancement, especially in recent generations, contrasting it with the slow technological change experienced by our ancestors. Key technologies, like artificial intelligence, are posited as having the potential for transformative impacts on society. The article stresses the importance of being informed about which technologies shape our future and who controls them, advocating for active participation in technological discussions.
