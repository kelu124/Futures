## Summary

The AI backlash is escalating amid rapid industry growth, exemplified by Anthropic's projected profits. Public sentiment is increasingly negative, with instances of booing speakers promoting AI at college events. Young people express distrust towards AI's promises, highlighting tangible issues like worker displacement, rising energy costs, and environmental concerns from data center construction. These immediate grievances overshadow optimistic narratives from AI leaders about future advancements. The polarization around AI has also united both political parties against data centers. While companies continue to invest in AI, the mounting public resistance suggests a brewing cultural conflict. There remains hope for a healthy integration of AI into society, although the process seems fraught with challenges and potential backlash.
