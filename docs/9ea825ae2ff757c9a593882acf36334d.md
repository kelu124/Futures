## Summary

The text discusses the importance of carving out a 'sacred hour' each day amidst the chaos of modern life, where individuals often feel overwhelmed by responsibilities and distractions. It emphasizes that dedicating this hour to personal growth and reflection can enhance productivity and well-being. The author suggests four tactics to free up time for this sacred hour: zero-based calendaring, delegation, reducing meeting frequencies, and compressing task time. By prioritizing self-care, individuals can better manage their obligations and lead more fulfilling lives.
