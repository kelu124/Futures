## Summary

The article discusses the trend of hyper-personalization in modern culture, driven by technology and societal shifts from communal to individualistic values. It illustrates this phenomenon with the TikTok 'Bean Soup' meme, which highlights the expectation for content to cater to individual preferences. The piece traces the evolution of individualism in American culture, citing increasing use of the word 'I' and changes in family structures. It argues that technology, especially the rise of AI, enhances personalization across various sectors, leading to tailored consumer experiences in health, fashion, and media. The author suggests that the 21st Century marks a shift from mass consumption to bespoke consumption, where products and services are increasingly customized to individual needs.
