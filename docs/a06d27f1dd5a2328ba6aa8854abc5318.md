## Summary

In the updated version of "10 Career Lessons" by Rishad Tobaccowala, he shares essential insights for navigating a career spanning decades. Key lessons include starting with the least undesirable job to build foundational skills, aligning career choices with industry trends, and planning for long-term career horizons rather than short-term gains. He emphasizes the importance of resilience, continuous self-improvement, and choosing the right mentors. As one progresses, specializing in areas of passion and comparative advantage becomes crucial. In the later stages of a career, unlearning outdated practices and managing exits gracefully is vital. Tobaccowala advocates for building a portfolio career that includes mentoring and giving back, ensuring a fulfilling transition into new phases of life.
