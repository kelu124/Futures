## Summary

In 2023, artificial intelligence (AI) is set to transform productivity, marking a significant shift in its historical trajectory of hype and disappointment. With advancements in deep learning, AI will empower ordinary individuals to complete complex tasks, such as creating marketing materials or generating emails, in mere minutes. This new era will see AI tools from established firms like OpenAI and innovative startups like Midjourney and Stability AI democratizing access to powerful technologies. The impact of AI will not be about replacing humans but about freeing up time and unleashing creativity, potentially sparking a wave of entrepreneurship. As businesses harness AI's capabilities, they will operate as if they have infinite skilled interns, fundamentally changing the landscape of production and innovation.
