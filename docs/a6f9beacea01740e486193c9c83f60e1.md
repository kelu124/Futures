## Summary

Slutcon is a unique retreat designed for sexually inexperienced men to learn intimacy and flirting skills, emphasizing a safe environment where they can practice interactions with willing women, referred to as "flirtees." The event features workshops, seminars, and social gatherings aimed at easing social anxieties related to courting women. Attendees like Simon, who had little real-world experience with intimacy, reported breakthroughs in understanding their own desires and fears. The retreat aims to address mounting sexual disconnection in younger generations. While the event promotes radical empathy and non-judgment, concerns about safety and boundary violations linger, highlighting the delicate balance between fostering intimacy and ensuring respect. Ultimately, participants leave with heightened confidence and new perspectives on romantic possibilities.
