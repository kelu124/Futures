## Summary

The text outlines subscription options for Standard Digital and Premium Digital access to Financial Times (FT) journalism. Subscribers receive complete digital access to global news, expert analysis, newsletters, videos, and podcasts. Key features include the FT App, FirstFT daily stories, curated newsletters, gift articles, and investment columns. Premium access offers additional benefits such as exclusive analyses, a markets data widget, and subscription management tools. Subscribers can save 20% by paying a year upfront and enjoy both digital and print editions, including FT Weekend delivery.
