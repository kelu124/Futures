## Summary

The article outlines 10 key learnings from a retreat attended by successful entrepreneurs in Big Sky, Montana. The insights emphasize that true success is about freedom rather than just financial wealth, the importance of a supportive environment, and the natural presence of insecurities among even the most accomplished individuals. Participants learned to recognize the games they play in life and the importance of creating value without expectations. Other points highlight the advantages of owning distribution through personal branding, the quiet nature of true success, the ongoing uncertainty in life, and the reality of entrepreneurial loneliness. Ultimately, the author stresses the significance of perspective in problem-solving and the value of surrounding oneself with ambitious, supportive individuals.
