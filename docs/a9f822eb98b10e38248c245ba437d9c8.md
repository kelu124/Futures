## Summary

The text discusses the profound impact of reading and writing on humanity, comparing the learning processes of AI language models with human experiences. It emphasizes that reading allows individuals to inhabit other lives, fostering empathy and understanding. Good writing resonates deeply, often changing readers in subtle ways. The author praises the accessibility of diverse voices in modern literature, highlighting the unique experience of reading physical books, which embody a tangible magic. Ultimately, the text argues that reading is integral to personal growth and connection, transcending the limits of time and space, and that books serve as artifacts rich in meaning and memories, influencing our lives significantly.
