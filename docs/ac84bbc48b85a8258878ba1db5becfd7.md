## Summary

The evolution of military technology, particularly artificial intelligence (AI) and unmanned systems, is reshaping warfare dynamics. Ukraine is investing in land robots, collaborating with U.S. firms to boost its defense manufacturing. The emphasis on AI in military operations raises strategic implications amid competition, primarily between the U.S. and China. Directed-energy weapons, like high-energy laser systems, are now being utilized by the U.S. Army for air defense, marking a historic transition. As global military spending surges due to rising tensions, the discourse around lethal autonomous weapons and ethical considerations in cyber warfare intensifies. The geopolitical landscape is shifting, with Europe enhancing defenses against perceived threats, particularly from Russia, while the U.S. navigates its relationship with a rising China.
