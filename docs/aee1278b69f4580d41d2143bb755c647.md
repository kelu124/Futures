## Summary

The text discusses the complexities surrounding the decision to follow one's passion in work, highlighting that the choice often depends on individual circumstances and goals. While pursuing personal interests can lead to fulfilling careers, especially in entrepreneurship, it may not always be financially viable. The author suggests that many successful startups originated from founders working on projects they were passionate about. The text also emphasizes the importance of exploring various interests to gain clarity about what one truly enjoys and excels at. Additionally, it advises making choices that preserve future options and considers the influence of colleagues on one's career path. Ultimately, for those aiming to do great work, pursuing one's interests is essential, as genuine curiosity fuels significant achievements.
