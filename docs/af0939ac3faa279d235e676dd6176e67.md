## Summary

In Edition #125 of Gorick's newsletter, career advisor Gorick Ng reflects on what can be learned from Taylor Swift's career journey. Swift's move at a young age to Nashville, where she aimed to get noticed by a record label, exemplifies the importance of being at the 'center of gravity' in one's field. Ng highlights a recent WalletHub study ranking the best cities to start a career, emphasizing how geographical choices can significantly impact career opportunities. He encourages readers to consider relocations for professional growth and shares advice for both early career individuals and those feeling restless in their current situations. The newsletter concludes with a reminder about the unspoken rules of career success, promoting his book and other offerings related to career development.
