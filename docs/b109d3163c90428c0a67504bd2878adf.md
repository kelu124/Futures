## Summary

The article discusses the decline of traditional SEO in favor of AI tools that provide synthesized answers to user queries. Unlike search engines, which rank information sources, AI generates clear, tailored responses, enhancing user productivity and satisfaction. Research indicates that users are more productive and prefer AI for questions, with AI narrowing the knowledge gap between educated and less-educated users. The author highlights the superiority of AI in providing comprehensive answers, supplemented by additional relevant information. As users increasingly turn to AI, the article suggests that websites should prepare for a significant decrease in SEO-driven traffic.
