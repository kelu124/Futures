## Summary

Klarna has launched an AI assistant powered by OpenAI, which has been operational for a month, successfully handling 2.3 million customer service interactions. This AI is equivalent to 700 full-time agents, achieving high customer satisfaction and a 25% reduction in repeat inquiries. Customers now resolve issues in under 2 minutes, a significant improvement from the previous 11 minutes. The assistant is available in 23 markets, supports over 35 languages, and is projected to improve Klarna's profits by $40 million in 2024. It enhances the shopping experience for 150 million users by managing tasks like refunds and financial insights while still allowing interaction with live agents. Klarna aims to utilize AI to improve efficiency in the retail banking sector and society as a whole.
