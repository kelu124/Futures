## Summary

The author emphasizes the numerous physical and mental benefits of a regular exercise routine, highlighting its role as a morning cornerstone in their life. By observing the return on investment (ROI) of exercise, they estimate that dedicating one year to exercise could potentially lead to a lifespan increase of 10 years, marking a 1:10 ratio. While acknowledging that multiple factors influence longevity, the author argues that exercise should not be viewed as wasted time since it brings joy, fulfillment, and a host of benefits including better sleep, strength, cognitive function, and community engagement. Ultimately, they encourage readers to start exercising incrementally, reinforcing that the positive impacts of physical activity are accessible at any age and throughout life.
