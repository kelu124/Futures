## Summary

Pernod Ricard has unveiled its 2050 Future Scenarios, focusing on how climate change will influence social interactions and consumer behavior. The study emphasizes the importance of convivialité, or human connection, and aims to prepare for environmental and societal shifts. By exploring multiple future scenarios, Pernod Ricard seeks to foster resilience and initiate discussions within its ecosystem. The company is actively engaging its employees in understanding the impacts of these scenarios on daily life and integrating them into its strategic planning. Ultimately, the goal is to achieve eco-harmony as the most desirable future outcome, promoting a collective approach to addressing climate challenges.
