## Summary

The article discusses an experience inspired by Rob Walker's audio essay on the Waking Up app, which encourages people to notice the unnoticed aspects of their surroundings during walks. The author applies this advice in Lisbon, focusing on the shadows and the small green plants that grow through cracks in sidewalks and walls. This exploration leads to a realization about the persistence of nature, as the author observes an abundance of plants thriving in seemingly inhospitable urban environments. The author finds this perspective uplifting and encourages others to adopt a similar approach to enhance their awareness of everyday beauty.
