## Summary

The article explores the history of Juicy Fruit gum, noting its significance in retail evolution and its unique branding. Introduced in 1893 by William Wrigley Jr. as his first commercial product, Juicy Fruit set the stage for future gum innovations like Wrigley’s Spearmint and Doublemint. The gum is famously associated with the term 'fascinating,' which has been used in its marketing since around 1930, evolving from 'Fascinating Flavor' to 'A Fascinating Artificial Flavor' by the 1940s. This shift reflects cultural attitudes toward synthetic flavors at the time, highlighting the product's intriguing backstory.
