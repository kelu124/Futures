## Summary

Al Michaels, the legendary sportscaster, will be providing daily personalized Olympic recaps on Peacock using AI-generated voice technology. Users can personalize their experience by entering their name, selecting up to three sports, and two types of highlights, resulting in tailored recaps each morning. NBC emphasizes that a team of editors will review all content for quality assurance to avoid any inaccuracies. The AI voice was trained on Michaels' previous broadcasts and aims to deliver convincing content. The service will launch on July 27, with personalized recaps starting July 28, although availability on Android is still unconfirmed.
