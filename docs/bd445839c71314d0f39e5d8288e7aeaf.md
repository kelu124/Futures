## Summary

Fusion energy holds the potential for safe, sustainable, and low-carbon power, necessitating advancements in understanding complex physics and engineering challenges. EUROfusion is harnessing Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to accelerate research, utilizing its extensive dataset from decades of fusion experiments. Recently, EUROfusion approved 15 AI and ML research projects, funded with â‚¬2.659 million, aimed at addressing key challenges in fusion research. Projects focus on improving energy confinement, accelerating simulations for plasma stability, and enhancing data analysis for better control of fusion processes. These initiatives illustrate the significant role AI and ML can play in advancing fusion energy technology and achieving effective control strategies.
