## Summary

The Deep Time Walk program offers a transformative walking experience that connects participants with Earth's 4.6 billion-year history through a 4.6km guided walk. It aims to inspire positive action for a regenerative planet by empowering a community of changemakers through resources like the Deep Time Cards, App, and Script. The program emphasizes intercultural storytelling to promote ecological flourishing and has received praise from notable figures for its unique blend of science and art. Supported by crowdfunding and partnerships, it seeks to deepen understanding of humanity's interconnectedness with nature.
