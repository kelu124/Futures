## Summary

In 2023, early-stage startup founders, investors, and builders noted significant changes in company-building tactics. Key insights identified a shift in growth strategies as traditional channels became saturated. Notable tactics for 2025 included an upgraded go-to-market playbook, the integration of AI agents acting as employees, and the emergence of 'tiny teams' capable of rapidly scaling with minimal staff, leading to predictions of unicorns being built with just three individuals. Additionally, there was a focus on developing defensibility in products and an increased interest in network effects, reflected in the updates to related manuals and resources. This evolving landscape represents the contemporary strategies for successful startups.
