## Summary

Christie’s is set to feature a significant sketch by Eugène Delacroix for his renowned painting "Liberty Leading the People" in its upcoming 19th Century European & Orientalist Art Sale in London. The sketch, expected to sell for £700,000-1,000,000, represents a key moment in French history, depicting Liberty as a powerful figure leading the people during the July Revolution of 1830. Delacroix's work is celebrated for its innovative use of light and form, influencing future art movements. The auction will include 84 lots, with other notable pieces by artists such as Gustave Courbet and Alberto Pasini.
