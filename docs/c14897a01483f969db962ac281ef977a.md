## Summary

In Cedar Rapids, Iowa, realtor JJ Johannes has leveraged ChatGPT, an AI chatbot, to streamline real estate listings, creating a four-bedroom home description in under five seconds. Many agents, including Andres Asion and Frank Trelles, have begun using ChatGPT to draft legal documents and calculate mortgage payments, significantly reducing their workload. While the AI tool is currently free, its potential for a subscription model raises concerns among some agents. Despite its limitations, such as occasional inaccuracies in basic math, ChatGPT is seen as a valuable asset in the real estate industry, saving time and enhancing efficiency. Experts note that while AI applications in real estate are not new, the adoption of tools like ChatGPT may signal a broader shift towards integrating AI in the industry.
