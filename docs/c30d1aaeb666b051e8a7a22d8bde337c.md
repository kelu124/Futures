## Summary

Bytebase, a 20-member team spread across four cities, emphasizes the importance of tools in enhancing productivity, similar to how Figma and GitHub have transformed design and development processes. They share their extensive list of over 30 SaaS services utilized for R&D, marketing, and administration, including GitHub, Linear, Figma, and Google Workspace. The team's monthly spending on these tools totals $1,183. They highlight their efficiency, with R&D releasing new versions bi-weekly and the marketing team producing multiple articles weekly. Despite occasional missteps in tool selection, Bytebase aims to provide insights into their choices for the benefit of other startups.
