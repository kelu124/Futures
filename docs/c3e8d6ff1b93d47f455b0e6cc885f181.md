## Summary

Unexplained drone sightings over New Jersey and other northeastern US regions have caused concern among Congress members and prompted calls for a limited state of emergency. New Jersey Senator Jon Bramnick has requested a ban on drones until the situation is clarified. Governor Phil Murphy held a briefing with federal officials, stating no known threat to public safety exists. The FBI has received over 3,000 reports but lacks explanations. Speculation arises over whether the sightings result from confusion or deliberate actions. Drones have been seen near sensitive locations, raising alarms among officials, while the Pentagon denies claims of foreign involvement.
