## Summary

Advances in large language models, such as ChatGPT, have revolutionized language processing by machines. This has opened up new research opportunities and raised questions about how to make the most of these advancements and address the associated risks. A course is being offered that combines workshops and seminars to explore these topics further. Students can choose to complete a project or produce a research project proposal focused on the main areas identified by the course. The schedule includes sessions on background information, evaluation methods, consensus building, emergent abilities of language models, evaluating human-model interactions, AI-mediated communication, and societal impacts of language models. The course aims to foster critical thinking and practical skills in utilizing and understanding language models.
