## Summary

The article explores the motivations behind tech billionaires like Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, and Jeff Bezos supporting Donald Trump, suggesting it goes beyond financial incentives to a deeper ideological belief in their superiority and a desire for unchecked power. This ideology, inspired by science fiction and transhumanism, promotes the idea that they are 'supermen' above societal rules and morality. The piece also discusses their ambitions for creating independent 'startup cities' and using cryptocurrency to escape democratic governance, highlighting a troubling alliance with Trump that undermines democratic principles and promotes a 'post-truth' reality.
