# __Exploring the Creative Potential of DALL-E 2__, from ([20220824](https://kghosh.substack.com/p/20220824).)

__[External link](https://hpjansson.org/blag/2022/08/16/adventure-game-graphics-with-dall-e-2/)__



## Summary

This article explores the author's experience using OpenAI's DALL-E 2 instance for creative work, specifically in generating game graphics for a point-and-click adventure game. The author discusses the strengths and limitations of DALL-E, highlighting the need for human guidance and careful prompts to achieve desired results. The process of pixelization using external tools is explained, along with the importance of style prompts and selection in generating suitable images. The author also touches on retouching and editing techniques to refine the generated images. The article delves into the economics and legal considerations of using DALL-E, as well as ethical concerns. Overall, the author presents a comprehensive exploration of using AI-generated images for game development.
