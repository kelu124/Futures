## Summary

China is developing a new chatbot based on President Xi Jinping's philosophies and various major internet databases. The chatbot, currently undergoing internal testing by the Cyberspace Research Institute, allows citizens to inquire about socialism and engage in intelligent conversations. It was trained using Xi's political philosophy, including his book on socialism, alongside multiple professional knowledge bases from the internet. The AI will be hosted on a dedicated server and is designed to perform tasks such as text summarization and translation. The initiative reflects a trend of creating AI replicas of historical figures for educational purposes.
