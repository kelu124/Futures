## Summary

Futures Canvas is a collaborative platform designed for tackling complex future-oriented challenges by harnessing collective intelligence. Users can set up and customize challenges, launch stakeholder surveys, crowdsource project ideas, and engage a diverse community in shaping the future. Benefits include an engaging environment, flexibility for participant contributions, the ability to gather insights efficiently, and integration of AI for analytics and visual content. Participants can join ongoing challenges, such as those initiated by the Berlin Science Week, which focus on innovative ways to enhance science engagement. Various organizations across sectors have successfully utilized Futures Canvas for their initiatives.
