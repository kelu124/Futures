## Summary

The Future Risks Report's 11th edition emphasizes the growing complexity of emerging risks affecting daily life, based on a survey of 3,000 experts and 20,000 individuals. Climate change remains the foremost concern globally, with 77% of the public feeling vulnerable. Geopolitical instability has risen as a significant worry, linked to ongoing conflicts in the Middle East and Ukraine. Cybersecurity is highlighted as a major threat, dropping to third place but still a top concern for many, influenced by geopolitical tensions and advancements in AI. The report underscores the impact of crises on daily life, the dangers of misinformation for political institutions, and the critical role of insurers in mitigating emerging risks.
