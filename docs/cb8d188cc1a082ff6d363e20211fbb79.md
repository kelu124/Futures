## Summary

The article explores the obsession of men with YouTube, highlighting comedian Johnny Gaffney's experience of falling asleep to male-centric true crime content and its impact on his relationships. YouTube has become a prevalent viewing platform, often intruding on personal lives and intimacy. Many men, despite knowing it may hinder connections with their partners, turn to YouTube for comfort and entertainment, often at bedtime. Shared viewing can create bonding moments, but it can also lead to feelings of neglect in relationships. Ultimately, the article examines the addictive nature of YouTube, revealing its role as a significant part of modern male leisure activities with a mix of both educational and trivial content.
