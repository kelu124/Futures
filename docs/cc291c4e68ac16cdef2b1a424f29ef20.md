## Summary

Co-Intelligence by Ethan Mollick is a comprehensive guide that explores the implications, capabilities, and ethics of generative AI. Scheduled for release on April 2, 2024, this book is highly praised by notable figures such as Angela Duckworth, Eric Horvitz, and Reid Hoffman for its clarity and practicality. Mollick provides insights into how AI can be harnessed for work and education, emphasizing its potential to enhance team capabilities and foster a more intelligent future. The book is positioned as an essential resource for leaders and anyone seeking to understand the rapidly evolving landscape of AI technology.
