## Summary

The author reflects on the nuances of AI's impact on skills and human productivity during a relaxed summer period. Contrary to the notion that AI makes technical skills obsolete, the author argues that expertise is becoming even more vital as AI becomes widespread, emphasizing the need for deep specialization and customization. Additionally, the author criticizes the trend of augmenting human capabilities, advocating instead for personal and inner growth. Finally, the piece highlights the true value of AI as a tool for shifting perspectives and enhancing curiosity rather than merely replicating human thought processes. The author concludes with a wish for a peaceful summer, signaling a pause in publishing.
