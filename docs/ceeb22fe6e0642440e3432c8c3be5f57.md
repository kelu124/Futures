## Summary

The text explores the interconnected themes of air, ecosystems, and the relationship between humans and invertebrates through the lens of art and science, particularly focusing on the works of Tomás Saraceno. It discusses various concepts such as Aerocene, a post-fossil fuel era, and the significance of spider webs as metaphors for connection and communication among life forms. The Arachnomancy Cards and the corresponding app serve as tools for understanding these relationships, highlighting topics like invertebrate rights and climate change. The overarching message emphasizes the radical interconnectedness of all beings, urging a deeper awareness of our shared environment and the need for collective action to address ecological challenges.
