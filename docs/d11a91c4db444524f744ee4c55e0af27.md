## Summary

The text introduces Roc Camera, a new photography device designed to capture verifiably real moments in an era increasingly dominated by AI and digitally altered images. It highlights the nostalgic essence of traditional photography, emphasizing the importance of reality in a world filled with edited images. Roc Camera aims to restore this authenticity by using advanced technology, including zero-knowledge proofs, to ensure the integrity of captured photos. The product is currently in beta testing, with preorders available for consumers interested in shaping its future. Expected shipping is slated for late 2024 or early 2025.
