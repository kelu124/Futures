## Summary

The text presents 663 predictions for the year 2030, outlining significant changes across various sectors including culture, technology, science, health, and business. Key forecasts include the prohibition of combustion fuel cars in Germany in favor of electric vehicles, advancements in medical technology allowing paralyzed individuals to regain hand function, and the development of universal flu vaccines. It anticipates a global population of approximately 8.5 billion and highlights the rise of autonomous vehicles and connected devices. Additionally, cultural and scientific shifts are predicted, such as changes in job structures due to automation and advancements in renewable energy. The insights come from Quantumrun Foresight, a consulting firm focused on strategic foresight for future trends.
