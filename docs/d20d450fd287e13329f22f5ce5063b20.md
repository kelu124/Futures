## Summary

OpenAI is focusing on audio AI as part of a shift towards audio-centered technology, anticipating the launch of an audio-first personal device within a year. The tech industry is increasingly leaning towards audio interfaces, with smart speakers and innovations from companies like Meta and Google leading the way. Various startups also aim to capitalize on this trend, despite mixed results. OpenAI's upcoming audio model, set for early 2026, aims for more natural interactions and may include a range of devices that prioritize companionship over traditional screen reliance. This direction aligns with a broader vision to reduce device addiction, influenced by former Apple design chief Jony Ive's efforts.
