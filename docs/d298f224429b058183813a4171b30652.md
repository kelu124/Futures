## Summary

The Long Table is a restaurant located in Stroud, South West England that operates on a pay-as-you-can model. They offer meals to community members and allow them to pay what they can afford. The restaurant focuses on providing accessible food and reducing food waste by using still-good food rescued from landfills. The Long Table also emphasizes conversation and community among its patrons, with long tables that encourage people to sit across from each other and engage in conversation. Despite the possibility of eviction, the restaurant has garnered strong community support.
