# __Technologies of Hope: Pandemic Solutions__, from ([20221002](https://kghosh.substack.com/p/20221002).)

__[External link](https://techpandemic.theglassroom.org/#)__



## Summary

This text discusses the role of technology and data-driven solutions in mitigating the pandemic and helping societies return to normal. It highlights various technologies such as quarantine bracelets, pandemic drones, predictive cleaning, bio-patches, and syndromic surveillance that have been developed to address pandemic-related problems. The text raises questions about the trade-offs and implications of relying on these technologies and emphasizes the importance of understanding the societies we are building. It explores the narratives, values, and propositions of technology companies and their promotion of these pandemic technologies. Ultimately, it points out the dilemma of whether our technological response offers greater control and understanding of the virus or of ourselves.
