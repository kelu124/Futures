## Summary

At two public meetings in Berlin and London, David North, chairperson of the World Socialist Web Site, addressed the crisis of American capitalism and the rise of Trump’s dictatorship, emphasizing the need for a revolutionary movement. He announced the launch of Socialism AI, a chatbot designed to enhance socialist consciousness among the international working class by providing detailed and accurate responses on political and social issues, unlike existing biased chatbots. North drew parallels between Socialism AI and historical educational tools, arguing it could democratize access to Marxist thought. The meetings reflected a growing interest in socialism as a solution to capitalism's crises, with enthusiastic responses from attendees.
