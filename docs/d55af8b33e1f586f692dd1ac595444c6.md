## Summary

The author shares their experience of using GPT-3 as a journaling tool for personal development over the past few weeks. They highlight the benefits of AI-assisted journaling, which provides a conversational approach that overcomes common journaling hurdles such as writer's block and monotony. GPT-3 offers the advantages of a supportive friend or coach, being available 24/7 and capable of summarizing and reframing thoughts. The author experimented with various personas, including Socrates and therapeutic modalities, noting both the successes and limitations of this method. They built a web app to enhance the experience by retaining conversation history and facilitating interactions. While acknowledging potential concerns about the authenticity of AI interactions, the author believes that as people grow accustomed to such tools, the stigma may diminish. They conclude by encouraging both seasoned and new journalers to explore GPT-3 as a valuable journaling alternative.
