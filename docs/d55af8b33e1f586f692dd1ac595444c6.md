# __GPT-3 as a Powerful Journaling Tool__, from ([20230122](https://kghosh.substack.com/p/20230122).)

__[External link](https://every.to/chain-of-thought/gpt-3-is-the-best-journal-you-ve-ever-used)__



## Summary

This text discusses the author's experience using GPT-3 for journaling and personal development. The author explains that GPT-3 provides many benefits similar to traditional journaling, such as creating a written record and offering support. GPT-3 can even mimic the support of a coach or therapist. The author explores different ways to use GPT-3 for journaling, including adopting different personas and exploring therapy modalities. While there are limitations and challenges, such as the need for better chat interfaces and avoiding repetitive questions, the author finds value in using GPT-3 as a journaling tool. The author believes that as people become more familiar with these tools, the objections to interacting with AI will diminish, and they will play a larger role in personal development practices.
