# __Building a Galactic Compass App with ChatGPT__, from ([20230331](https://kghosh.substack.com/p/20230331).)

## Summary

This text is about the creation of an app called "Galactic Compass" that features a green floating arrow that always points towards the middle of the Milky Way, specifically the supermassive black hole Sagittarius A*. The author explains that despite not knowing how to write apps, they were able to develop the app by using ChatGPT, an artificial intelligence model, to guide them through the development process. The app involves astronomical calculations, 3D graphics, and combining rotations. The author emphasizes the collaboration between themselves and ChatGPT, highlighting the benefits of using AI tools in the creative process. The text also discusses the concept of an app being a "home-cooked meal" and the potential of AI to enhance the skills of individuals. The author shares links to download the app and provides updates on its popularity and media coverage.
