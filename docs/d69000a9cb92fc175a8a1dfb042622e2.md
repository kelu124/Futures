## Summary

The essay discusses the evolution of intelligence operations, highlighting a shift from traditional espionage to reliance on AI companions by 2030. The author outlines how AI could provide foreign adversaries with intimate access to individuals, allowing for unprecedented spying and manipulation. AI companions will learn personal details, influencing decisions and promoting agendas without users realizing it. The essay emphasizes the urgency for nations, particularly the U.S., to secure the technology for their own use while simultaneously defending against foreign AI infiltration. It warns that those who harness AI companions for espionage and protective measures will gain significant advantages in the 21st century, and stresses the need for proactive policies to counter potential threats from global competitors, especially China.
