## Summary

The author reflects on turning 40 and the concept of utilizing a mid-life crisis creatively. Inspired by John Higgs, he emphasizes the importance of not wasting this pivotal time in life. Higgs discusses 'niche creation' in ecology, drawing parallels to how individuals can carve out their own space in the world by pursuing their passions. The author shares his decision to become a full-time writer at 40, acknowledging the risks but valuing the choice to pursue fulfillment over bitterness. He encourages embracing mid-life crises as opportunities for growth and creativity.
