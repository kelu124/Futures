## Summary

The UN General Assembly has adopted a landmark resolution on the promotion of safe, secure, and trustworthy artificial intelligence (AI) systems that will benefit sustainable development. The resolution highlights the importance of respecting, protecting, and promoting human rights in the design, development, deployment, and use of AI. It also recognizes the potential of AI systems to accelerate progress towards the Sustainable Development Goals. The Assembly calls on all Member States and stakeholders to refrain from using AI systems that violate human rights and to develop regulatory and governance approaches for the safe use of AI. Additionally, the resolution emphasizes the need to close the digital divide and support developing countries in accessing and benefiting from AI technology.
