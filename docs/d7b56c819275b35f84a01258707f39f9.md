## Summary

The UN General Assembly adopted a historic resolution aimed at ensuring safe and trustworthy artificial intelligence (AI) systems that contribute to sustainable development. Supported by over 120 member states, the resolution emphasizes the need for AI to respect human rights throughout its lifecycle. It calls for a halt to the use of AI systems that do not comply with international human rights laws and urges cooperation to support developing countries in closing the digital divide. The resolution represents a significant step towards global governance of AI technology, aiming to ensure it serves humanity's interests while promoting inclusive dialogue on AI challenges in various sectors.
