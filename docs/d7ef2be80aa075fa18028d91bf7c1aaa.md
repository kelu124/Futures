## Summary

The text discusses the necessity of adapting to rapid changes in the workplace and employment landscape driven by technology, unbundled work, and global marketplaces. It highlights the significant job losses in the tech and media sectors and emphasizes the importance of developing six key skills for future success: Cognition, Creativity, Curiosity, Collaboration, Communication, and Convincing. These skills will help individuals thrive alongside AI and in a connected world, ensuring they remain relevant and capable of navigating the evolving job market. The article stresses that proactive investment in personal development is essential to embrace the future effectively.
