## Summary

In 2022, the top-earning dead celebrities collectively earned a record $1.6 billion, a 72% increase from the previous year. J.R.R. Tolkien topped the list with $500 million from the sale of Middle-Earth Enterprises, followed by Kobe Bryant with $400 million from his stake in BodyArmor, and David Bowie at $250 million for his publishing catalog. Elvis Presley generated $110 million mainly from Graceland tours. The music industry continues to see substantial earnings from deceased artists, with nearly $700 million generated from musicians alone. The report highlights a shift in intellectual property control, emphasizing the resurgence of artists' estates as significant revenue sources in the entertainment world.
