## Summary

Goldman Sachs is testing an AI software engineer named Devin, created by the startup Cognition, to augment its workforce of 12,000 developers. Expected to handle tasks such as coding and updating internal systems, Devin represents a shift in corporate AI adoption, capable of boosting productivity significantly compared to previous tools. The move towards integrating such AI raises concerns about job cuts, with projections estimating potential losses in the banking sector due to automation. However, Goldman Sachs envisions a "hybrid workforce" where humans and AI collaborate, enhancing productivity and redefining roles within the industry.
