## Summary

The demo titled 'AI Chat... But Make It Ads' offers a satirical look at an AI chat interface supported entirely by advertisements. It showcases various monetization strategies including pre-chat ads, sponsored responses, and freemium models. The creator, Nick Kirtley, aims to illustrate the potential future of ad-supported AI chats, emphasizing the trade-offs between free access and user privacy. The demo features fictional brands and ads, demonstrating how these patterns may affect the user experience. It's intended as an educational tool for marketers and developers, providing insights into the landscape of AI monetization and inviting discussions about the desired future of AI products.
