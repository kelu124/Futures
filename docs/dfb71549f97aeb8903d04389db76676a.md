## Summary

The Enron Egg is a compact nuclear reactor designed for residential use, utilizing Uranium-Zirconium Hydride fuel rods for efficient power generation. Its heat-resistant casing and heavy water pump ensure safety and optimal performance under extreme conditions. The reactor features real-time monitoring and advanced safety mechanisms, including automatic reaction halting and radiation shielding. The Egg is significantly more affordable than traditional energy systems, providing a reliable and constant energy source with low long-term costs. Preorders are available for those interested in this innovative energy solution.
