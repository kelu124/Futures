## Summary

This text explores the concept of foresight and its impact on various aspects of human life, ranging from the environment to scientific advancements. It highlights the consequences of failing to foresee the outcomes of certain actions, such as the tragic fire at Krefeld Zoo caused by sky lanterns, and the disastrous release of balloons in Cleveland. The text emphasizes the need for improved foresight to mitigate the negative effects of human activities on the planet, including pollution, climate change, and biodiversity loss. It also showcases the positive outcomes of foresight, such as advancements in medicine and conservation efforts like the rezoning of the Great Barrier Reef. Overall, the text underscores the importance of foresight in shaping a sustainable and desirable future.
