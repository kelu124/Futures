## Summary

The tragic incident at Krefeld Zoo in Germany, where sky lanterns released by a family ignited a fire that killed numerous primates, highlights the unpredictable consequences of seemingly innocent actions. This event symbolizes broader issues concerning human foresight and its failures. While foresight has allowed humanity to innovate and improve life quality through advancements in science and technology, it has also led to environmental degradation and unforeseen disasters. The text emphasizes the importance of learning from past mistakes, adopting systematic approaches to problem-solving, and recognizing the moral implications of our choices. Ultimately, harnessing foresight effectively could help create a sustainable future for both humanity and nature.
