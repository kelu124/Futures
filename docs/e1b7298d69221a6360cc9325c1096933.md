## Summary

The construction industry is embracing digital technology, with 76% of executives planning investments to enhance processes and stay competitive. Key technologies transforming the sector include Data Collection Apps, Drones, Building Information Modeling (BIM) Software, Virtual Reality, 3D Printing, and Artificial Intelligence. Data Collection Apps improve data accuracy and safety compliance, while Drones enable swift site surveys and inspections. BIM fosters collaboration and efficiency in project planning. Virtual Reality enhances project visualization, and Wearables boost safety and productivity. 3D Printing streamlines material sourcing, while AI advances workflows and safety measures. Investing in these technologies can significantly benefit construction companies, providing better workflows and competitive advantages.
