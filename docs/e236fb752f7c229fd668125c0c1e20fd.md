## Summary

The text discusses the unpredictability of the future, particularly looking ahead to 2026, amidst a backdrop of political upheaval and technological advancements. It highlights the annual Nesta collection that identifies signals indicating possible future trends, including the rise of 'sand crime' due to construction demands on specific types of sand, innovations in GPS technology using quantum advancements to counter signal disruptions, and the exploration of human reproduction in space as part of future colonization efforts. The collection also touches on other developments like AI governance and crowd-sourced public transport solutions. An invitation to the 2026 Future Signals event in London is included, promoting networking and discussions about upcoming innovations.
