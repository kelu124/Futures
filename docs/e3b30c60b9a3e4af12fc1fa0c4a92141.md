## Summary

The Travel Issue of Tank Magazine, 2021 (Issue 89), explores the themes of globalization and the notion of 'elsewhere' through various photography and styling features. It includes articles by notable contributors like Masoud Golsorkhi and Matthew Janney, covering diverse topics such as Swiss culture, modern audio experiences, and the impacts of colonialism. The magazine showcases a range of visual narratives, from urban landscapes to intimate ceremonies, and engages with contemporary issues through interviews with artists and thinkers like Fintan O'Toole and Flora Yin-Wong. Overall, the issue invites readers to reflect on travel, identity, and the complexities of modern existence.
