## Summary

The article discusses the transition from a knowledge economy to an allocation economy in the age of AI. With the advancements in AI, tasks like summarizing are being handed over to AI models like ChatGPT, changing the skills required in the workforce. The author explores the qualities that model managers in the allocation economy would need, such as a coherent vision, a clear sense of taste, the ability to evaluate talent, and knowing when to get into the details. The article also raises questions about the impact of the allocation economy on society and the need to ensure the inclusion of the entire economy in this transition. Overall, it highlights the changing dynamics of work and the role of AI in shaping the future of work.
