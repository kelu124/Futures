## Summary

French startup Mistral AI has successfully closed a Series A funding round, raising €385 million (approximately $415 million), valuing the company at around $2 billion. Co-founded by former Google and Meta employees, Mistral AI aims to establish a European competitor to OpenAI by developing foundational models with an open technology approach. The funding round, led by Andreessen Horowitz, includes participation from notable investors such as Salesforce and BNP Paribas. Mistral AI has released its first model, Mistral 7B, as a free download, promoting accessibility for developers. Additionally, it is opening a beta developer platform for paid access to its models via APIs. Mistral AI has been active in discussions regarding the EU’s AI Act, advocating for foundational models to be exempt from certain regulations. It also introduced a new Mixtral 8x7B model, enhancing efficiency while managing costs and latency.
