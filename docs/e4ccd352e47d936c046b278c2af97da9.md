## Summary

The New York Times is establishing a team to investigate the application of generative AI in its newsroom, led by newly appointed AI initiatives head Zach Seward. The team will focus on prototyping AI and machine-learning techniques to enhance reporting and presentation to readers. Job postings for various roles, including a machine learning engineer and editorial director for AI initiatives, have been made. Despite previous tensions with generative AI, including a lawsuit against OpenAI, the Times emphasizes that its journalism will remain human-driven. Other news organizations are also exploring AI integration in their operations amid challenges related to misinformation.
