## Summary

Contrary is an investment firm that emphasizes the role of exceptional individuals in building iconic companies while exploring foundational technology trends and their secondary effects. Their annual Tech Trends Report highlights key technology currents and the social behavior they inspire, aiming to identify opportunities. The firm also conducts in-depth research on private tech companies to clarify trends in private markets, providing insightful analyses to better understand the industry. Contributors to their reports include several key researchers who support this mission.
