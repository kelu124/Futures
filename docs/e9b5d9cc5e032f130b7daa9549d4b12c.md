## Summary

ChatGPT has warned architects about the existential threat posed by AI, suggesting that they could become obsolete. In a conversation with AI architecture expert Neil Leach, ChatGPT stated that the rapid advancements in AI could allow it to autonomously generate building designs, potentially sidelining traditional architects. Leach expressed concern that architecture jobs are already at risk, as AI's efficiency and accuracy may revolutionize the industry. ChatGPT urged architects to embrace AI as a tool and adapt to avoid being forgotten. The rise of AI in architecture has become increasingly relevant, with significant investments from major tech companies in AI chatbot projects.
