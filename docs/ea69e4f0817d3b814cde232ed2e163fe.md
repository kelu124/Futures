## Summary

During the Q4 earnings call with investors, Apple CEO Tim Cook highlighted the importance of AI in the company's recent technology developments. He mentioned features like Personal Voice and Live Voicemail in iOS 17, which utilize AI technologies. Cook clarified that although these features are not explicitly labeled as AI by Apple, the underlying technology is AI and machine learning. Personal Voice, for instance, is an accessibility feature aimed at helping people with speech impairments, while Live Voicemail provides real-time transcription of voicemails. Cook also confirmed that Apple is actively working on generative AI technologies. Despite Apple's efforts, the company is considered to be catching up to other companies in terms of consumer-facing AI technologies. However, Apple has reportedly increased its investment in AI and is focused on integrating AI capabilities into Siri and various iOS features.
