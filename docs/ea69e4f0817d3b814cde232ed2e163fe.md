## Summary

During Apple's Q4 earnings call, CEO Tim Cook defended the company's position in AI, highlighting innovations in iOS 17, such as Personal Voice and Live Voicemail, which leverage AI technology without explicitly labeling them as such. Personal Voice is an accessibility feature for those losing their speech, while Live Voicemail offers real-time transcription. Cook emphasized that AI underpins many Apple features, including health-related functions on devices. He confirmed ongoing development in generative AI, stating significant investments are being made responsibly. Despite this progress, Apple is perceived to be lagging behind competitors in consumer-facing AI technologies, with plans to enhance Siri and integrate more AI capabilities into future iOS updates.
