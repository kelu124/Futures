## Summary

Mind Medicine Inc. announced that its LSD formulation, MM120, received FDA breakthrough therapy status for treating generalized anxiety disorder (GAD). This designation acknowledges significant clinical efficacy in addressing unmet medical needs. Results from a phase 2b trial showed a 48% remission rate from GAD at 12 weeks and significant improvement in symptoms for 65% of participants. The trial, which did not include psychotherapy, marked a return to LSD research after over 40 years, with MM120 manufactured to pharmaceutical standards. Adverse effects were generally mild. The study highlights the potential of psychedelics beyond depression treatments, offering hope for more effective anxiety treatments.
