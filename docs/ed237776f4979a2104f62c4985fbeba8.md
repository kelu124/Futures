## Summary

This article discusses the practicalities and challenges of adopting generative AI in businesses. While there is a growing appetite for generative AI, many businesses lack a clear strategy and understanding of how to effectively use AI. Custom B2B research is highlighted as a valuable investment that can give businesses an edge over their competitors. The article emphasizes that generative AI offers benefits beyond just increasing efficiency, such as parsing through large amounts of data and identifying patterns that humans would miss. Custom research is recommended to gain clarity on how to best implement generative AI and address customers' needs. The article concludes by urging businesses to be thoughtful and deliberate in incorporating AI into their operations.
