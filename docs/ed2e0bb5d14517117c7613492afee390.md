## Summary

The author shares their experience playing the game Sixty Four, which is available on Windows and Mac via Steam. The game engages players in a cycle of resource extraction, machine building, and optimization, starting from a blank space with a single Extracting Channel. As players progress, they automate processes and expand their operations, all while contemplating the nature of humanity and the purpose behind their actions. Despite its simplicity, the game captivates with its intricate systems and sound design. The author highlights the immersive nature of the gameplay, which can lead to distractions from real-life responsibilities, as well as hints at deeper themes within the game.
