## Summary

StolenTime St. Lucia has introduced a 'Grief Retreat' package, designed in collaboration with trauma expert Annalie Howling, to assist individuals and groups dealing with loss or life transitions. Launching ahead of National Grief Awareness Week, the retreat aims to provide a supportive environment for emotional recovery, addressing the needs of the 51% of bereaved individuals in the UK who report significant vulnerability. The program combines professional therapeutic support with the Caribbean's natural healing setting, featuring activities like mindfulness paddleboarding and Caribbean DanceFit, as well as specialized spa treatments.
