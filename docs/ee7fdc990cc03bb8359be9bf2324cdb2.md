## Summary

Futur(s) is a weekly newsletter that explores emerging future scenarios, reflecting on societal changes from a 'spinning top' model, representing a middle-class-dominated society, to an 'hourglass' model characterized by economic and cultural polarization. This shift indicates a reduction of the middle class, complicating social cohesion and collective projects. Emotions in this new hourglass society are increasingly outsourced to AI, leading to a dichotomy of invisibilized emotions and a craving for intense, authentic human experiences. The challenge lies in maintaining human connections amidst these shifts, raising crucial questions about emotional wellbeing in an evolving societal context.
