## Summary

The landscape of AI's impact on employment presents a stark contradiction, with studies showing job loss in certain sectors while others demonstrate job growth and wage increases in AI-exposed occupations. This uncertainty necessitates scenario planning—envisioning divergent futures based on the speed of AI capability development and adoption. Four scenarios emerge: the Augmentation Economy, where AI enhances job roles; the Slow Squeeze, with gradual job loss; the Displacement Crisis, featuring rapid job loss; and the Great Transformation, where new opportunities flourish. The way forward for employers, workers, and policymakers lies in choosing to leverage AI for growth and innovation rather than efficiency alone. Through careful planning and strategic choices, society can navigate the challenges and opportunities brought forth by AI.
