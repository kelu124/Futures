## Summary

Elba (eF#21) has successfully raised €2.5 million to focus on employee-centric cybersecurity solutions, inspired by models from firms like Netflix and Slack. This funding, led by Xange with contributions from notable security experts, aims to empower employees to manage cyber risks effectively. The founders, with previous experience in cybersecurity, recognized that traditional awareness training was insufficient, leading to disengagement and ineffective IT support. Elba’s innovative tool integrates with existing SaaS systems, alerting employees to security issues and guiding them through resolution steps. With over 100 clients and significant progress in addressing security challenges, Elba seeks to expand its user-focused approach globally, particularly targeting the US market, while further enhancing its research and development efforts.
