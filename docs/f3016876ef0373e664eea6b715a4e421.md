# __Work on What Really Matters__, from ([20220717](https://kghosh.substack.com/p/20220717).)

__[External link](https://lethain.com/work-on-what-matters/)__



## Summary

The text discusses the importance of focusing on meaningful work and prioritizing what matters in one's career. It emphasizes the limited amount of time individuals have to work and the need to balance work with other aspects of life. The text highlights the challenges of senior roles and the increasing expectations for high-impact work in less time. It advises against engaging in activities such as snacking, preening, and chasing ghosts, which can be low-impact and distracting. The text suggests focusing on existential issues, areas with room for growth, and nurturing the team around you. It also emphasizes the importance of finishing projects and engaging in work that only you can uniquely accomplish. Ultimately, the text emphasizes the value of doing work that matters and aligning with companies that value genuine expertise.
