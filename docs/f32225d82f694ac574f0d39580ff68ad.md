## Summary

OpenAI has launched the ChatGPT and Whisper models on its API, offering developers advanced language and speech-to-text capabilities with a significant cost reduction of 90% since December. Early adopters include Snap Inc., Quizlet, Instacart, and Shopify, who are integrating these technologies to enhance user experiences. The ChatGPT API, priced at $0.002 per 1k tokens, utilizes a new format called Chat Markup Language (ChatML), allowing for improved interactions. Additionally, OpenAI is now offering dedicated instances for developers needing greater control over performance. The Whisper API, available for $0.006 per minute, provides efficient speech-to-text services. OpenAI has also made several adjustments based on developer feedback, including data privacy improvements and a focus on service stability.
