## Summary

Google made several announcements at its annual developer conference, Google I/O, including new additions to its AI model series Gemini. The company introduced Gemini 1.5 Pro, which offers an extended context window, making it a foundational model with great potential. Google's focus on generative AI is evident, as it aims to provide consumers with more advanced and creative ways to access online information. Additionally, OpenAI also launched its own AI model, GPT-4o, which is faster and more cost-effective. Both Google and OpenAI are pushing the boundaries of AI development and offering developers new tools and interfaces to build innovative applications.
