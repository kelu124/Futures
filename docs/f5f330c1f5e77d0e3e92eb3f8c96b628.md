## Summary

The article discusses the importance of understanding one's personality in navigating careers, especially in the AI Age. It highlights the introduction of myTrudy, a free service that assesses individuals' Personality Quotient (PQ) in under five minutes. The founders emphasize that personal traits such as agreeableness, conscientiousness, extroversion, openness, and emotional stability are essential for future career success. Reinvention, adaptability, and persistence are crucial as the workforce evolves. The discussion contrasts traditional resumes with the need for personality insights in career planning. The article invites listeners to learn from the co-founders of myTrudy about shaping future careers amid shifting work dynamics.
