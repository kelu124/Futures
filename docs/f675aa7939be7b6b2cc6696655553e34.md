## Summary

The narrative describes the journey of two entrepreneurs, Oksana and the author, who find themselves in London after fleeing a war-torn Ukraine. Struggling to establish their footing, they create a quirky concept called 'Future Delivers,' a fake delivery company that sends parcels from the future containing advice and artifacts to influential individuals. Their approach includes innovative marketing strategies, such as targeting ads to deceased individuals on social media to reach their desired audience. Despite the challenges and humorous setbacks, they manage to engage several high-profile companies, receiving both interest and cease-and-desist letters. The story explores themes of creativity, resilience, and the unpredictable nature of entrepreneurship.
