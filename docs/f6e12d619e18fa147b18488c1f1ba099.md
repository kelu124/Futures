## Summary

The Girl with All the Gifts is a 2016 British post-apocalyptic science fiction horror film directed by Colm McCarthy and written by Mike Carey. It is based on Carey's book of the same name. The film takes place in a dystopian future where society has collapsed due to a fungal infection that turns most of humanity into mindless zombies. The story follows a scientist, a teacher, and two soldiers who are on a survival journey with a special young girl named Melanie. The film explores themes of post-apocalyptic survival and the consequences of scientific experimentation. It received positive reviews from critics.
