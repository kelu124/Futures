## Summary

The book "U.K. Crisp Packets 1970 - 2000" published by Sports Banger’s Banger Books, showcases the visual history of UK crisp packets from the 1970s to 2000. Compiled by Chris Packet and featuring an essay by Professor Annabella Pollen, it highlights the unique and nostalgic designs that shaped childhoods, featuring brands like Discos and Smuggler’s Bites. Chris, inspired by his interest in graphic design and culture, began collecting crisp packets in 2018 and aims to preserve their designs amid the age of AI. The book not only reflects the aesthetic trends of the time, linking them to pop culture but also serves as a tribute to the shared memories of the past.
