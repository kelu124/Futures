## Summary

Apple is testing heart rate measurement features in future AirPods models, specifically AirPods Pro 3 and the next generation Powerbeats Pro, as discovered in iOS 18. A recent patent application details the use of health sensors to not only track heart rate but also detect potential heart diseases through microphone signals. The new code suggests that users can wear both earbuds during workouts to monitor their heart rates and manage them via Bluetooth settings. The technology aims to identify various heart conditions, including arrhythmias and coronary artery disease, by analyzing heart activity signals. The AirPods Pro 3 are anticipated to be launched next year with these health monitoring capabilities.
