## Summary

A teenager discovers a ghost car in an old Xbox racing game he played with his late father. After avoiding the console for ten years following his dad's death, he returns to find that the game retains a record of his father's racing actions as a ghost driver. The son recalls the joy they shared playing together and feels a deep emotional connection when he surpasses the ghost car, choosing to preserve it at the finish line. This experience serves as a bittersweet reminder of his father's love, evoking both loss and comfort in the memories they created together.
