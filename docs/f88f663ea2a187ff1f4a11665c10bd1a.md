## Summary

The author reflects on the complexity of life trajectories, suggesting that many individuals often become obsessively focused on various interests, leading to unconventional paths. Reading history provides perspective, revealing that past lives were often much harder, and that varying experiences can lead to fulfillment. The societal rules for careers can feel restrictive, and many individuals may not fit into these molds due to their unique personalities. The essence of life, as conveyed, is the flexibility in defining success and finding personal satisfaction while adhering to fundamental responsibilities.
