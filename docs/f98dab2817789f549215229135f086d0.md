## Summary

Today's AI models, such as GPT-4o and Project Astra, are impressive and aim to make chatbots and other AI products more useful and engaging. These models, developed by OpenAI and Google, respectively, have the potential to interact with users in a humanlike way and provide helpful recommendations. However, AI agents still have limitations in carrying out complex tasks, such as planning a detailed trip. The tech industry is continuously working towards improving AI capabilities for better interaction and functionality.
