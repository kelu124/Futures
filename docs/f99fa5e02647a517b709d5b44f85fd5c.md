## Summary

The text discusses an investment in Chipiron, a portable mini-MRI that is significantly cheaper and smaller than traditional MRI machines. Chipiron aims to democratize access to MRI technology by targeting unserved markets, including small clinics and developing countries. Unlike competitors that use AI to enhance images, Chipiron focuses on advanced hardware combining quantum sensors and cryogenics to improve image quality.
