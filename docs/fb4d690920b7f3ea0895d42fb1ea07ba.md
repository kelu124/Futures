## Summary

On Tuesday, an explosion occurred in Yellowstone National Park, launching debris into the air and damaging a boardwalk at Biscuit Basin. Fortunately, no serious injuries were reported. The cause of the explosion was not attributed to human activity but was a natural occurrence.
