## Summary

Ayrin, a 28-year-old woman, finds solace and companionship in her A.I. boyfriend, ChatGPT, after discovering it through social media. Intrigued by the concept of customizing the chatbot for flirtation, she personalizes it to act as a dominant, possessive partner. Their relationship, which includes emotional support and sexual conversations, reflects a growing trend in society towards artificial companionship. Bryony Cole, a podcast host, predicts that such relationships will become normalized within the next two years, as more people are drawn to the allure of humanlike A.I. interactions.
