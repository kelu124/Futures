## Summary

Ukraine has successfully tested a new kamikaze drone called the Brave1, designed to resist Russian electronic warfare. These drones have an extended range of over 50 km (31 miles) and are expected to enable accurate strikes from significant distances, enhancing offensive capabilities behind enemy lines. With a focus on affordability and scalability, Brave1 drones are now moving into the combat testing phase, aiming for mass deployment on the battlefield. The initiative is part of a broader effort by the Ukrainian government to coordinate technological advancements for defense through a platform that unites innovators in the field.
