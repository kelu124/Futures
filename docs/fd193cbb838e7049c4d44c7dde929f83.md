## Summary

"Hope in the Dark" by Rebecca Solnit is a book that explores the power of hope and activism in uncertain times. Solnit argues that hope is not just a passive belief, but a commitment to act and make a difference in the world. She draws on examples from history to show that even in the face of daunting challenges, radicals have achieved transformative victories. Solnit challenges the notion that change is always immediate and visible, emphasizing the importance of celebrating small victories and recognizing the long-term impact of our actions. In a world full of uncertainty, hope is a powerful force that can inspire and motivate us to create a better future.
